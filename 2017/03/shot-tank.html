The shot (Hebrew :שוט‎(Sho't)) is the name of the tank attached for センチュリオン made in the U.K. which Israel remodeled. Large repair is carried out for the purpose of the smooth use in the desert zone in the Middle East and reinforcement of the ability for battle.

"The shot" means the "whip" "wrath of Heaven" in Hebrew, but the direct origin comes from the next one paragraph of "Nahum".

A whip. The sound of the wheel. The horse which gallops. The tank which flies, and runs.
