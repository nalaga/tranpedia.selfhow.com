Israel Premier League (Hebrew :ליגתהעלהישראלית‎, English: Israeli Premier League) is the top soccer league in Israel. In late years I am said to be Ligat Winner (Hebrew :ליגתווינר‎) than a sponsor name. [1]

Maccabi Haifa F.C., Hapoel Tel Aviv F.C., Beitar Jerusalem F.C., Maccabi Tel Aviv F.C. called big 4 perform a fight for championship in every season. Maccabi Haifa F.C. in particular goes into a UEFA champions league book war for the first time as a club in the State of Israel and accomplishes progress globally.

League summary

  • As for the 1999-2000 season of the league first year, I am comprised of 14 clubs 16 clubs 12 clubs 14 clubs from a 2012-13 season from a 2009-10 season to a 2011-12 season from a 2000-01 season to a 2008-09 season.
  • I begin in August and am usually held until May in the next year. There is not (winter break) during a winter interruption period.
  • I perform home and away method 26 games by 14 clubs and divide it into higher 6 club, the lower 8 club, and a high rank does a round-robin, a low rank once round-robin twice and decides the last order.
  • The participation right to the UEFA champions league of the next fiscal year is given the championship club.
  • The club of second place, third place and cup war championship is given the participation right of the UEFA Europe league.
  • Thirteenth place, club two copies of the 14th place are demoted automatically in the league (they are replaced, and two copies of league first place, club of the second place are promoted).
