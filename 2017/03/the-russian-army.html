The Russian army (Russian りくぐん, Russian :Сухопутныевойска) is the army in Russian Federales.

Table of contents

Summary

After the fashion of Soviet Union ground troops of the cold war era, I may be called Russian ground troops (Russian ちじょうぐん). As of 2016, hold military power and tank approximately 2,700 of approximately 270,000 people (approximately 17,500 other storage) [1]; (do not include the associate military affairs organizations such as the border patrol and Department of the Interior forces).

With the times when a military command engine of the army was not installed by the process that developed into the General Staff Office (in other words, the army as the military class did not exist) of the entire army same in Soviet Union Federales a military command engine of the army (the armed forces of the Communist bloc often imitate the example of the Soviet Union forces, too). From this, I call it with ground troops (ground force or land force) without calling an army organization of the Communist bloc army (army) in the west and refer it to ground troops in the Self-Defense Forces and the Korea forces (I refer the North Korean army built after the fashion of the Soviet Union forces to ground troops).

On the other hand, the literal translation of official name Сухопутныевойска in Russian is the army to the last. In Russian, it is normal that the ground troops are expressed by a totally different word Наземныевойска. Adjective in conjunction with "суша" where "сухопутный" means "the land", "наземный" are adjectives in conjunction with "земля" which "the earth" means, and it has unreasonableness closely to translate "Сухопутныевойска" as "ground troops" literally. History

The total number of Red Army troops became enormous, and an independent conduct organization was required during World War II every day because instruction became difficult, and, in March, 1946, the first army general headquarters were installed.

Because the demobilization of the call-up advanced, and the total number of army decreased, in March, 1950, the general headquarters were dissolved.

In March, 1955, the general headquarters were set up again, but were dissolved again in March, 1964. In November, 1967, the third general headquarters were set up.

After Soviet Union collapse, the general headquarters were reorganized as army total station (ГлавноеуправлениеСухопутныхвойск) for from November, 1997 to December by some police stations of the General Staff Office.

According to "a future figure of Russian Federales which Dmitry President Medvedev approved in 2008," reorganization from 4 laminar structure of "the district under jurisdiction of military - military - division - regiment" to 3 laminar structure of "the district under jurisdiction of military - strategy command - brigade" is performed, and it was said that I completed it by 2009, but 親衛 タマン motorization snipe division and 親衛 takeoff point Miro shark tank division participate in a victory memory parade in 2013, and a division revives in a part [2].

The combatant of the Russian army is divided into a combatant, a support combatant, backward military unit, facilities.

Combatant

The combatant (родавойск) is so-called battle type of job to participate in a battle directly.

  • A motorization sniper (Мотострелковыевойска): Foot soldier
  • Tanker (Танковыевойска)
  • A rocket, a gunner (Ракетныевойскаиартиллерия): Gunner
  • An air defense soldier (ВойсковаяПВО): High-angle gunner
  • Air Corps (Армейскаяавиация): The present is a combatant of the Russian air force

Support combatant

A support combatant (when it is специальныевойска, a literal translation, it becomes the Special Forces, but translates it as a support combatant here) is a combatant to guarantee duty accomplishment of the army.

  • A scout (разведывательныевойска): Information job area. The jurisdiction of Russian Federales General Staff Office information total station (GRU)
  • Communication soldier (войскасвязи)
  • Electric wave electronic warfare soldier (войскарадиоэлектроннойборьбы)
  • Military engineer (инженерныевойска)
  • Radiation, chemistry, biology protection soldier (войскарадиационной,химическойибиологическойзащиты)
  • Technical security soldier (войскатехническогообеспечения)
  • A car soldier (автомобильныевойска): Transportation soldier
  • Backward guard soldier (войскаохранытыла)

Backward military unit, facilities

It is common throughout the entire army, and posterior Russian Federales consolidates the backward military unit, facilities (воинскиечастииучреждениятыла) (because originally I developed from the organization of the army, even backward military unit, facilities duty person of the navy position is tinged with an army-type uniform and rank name).
