A lock bridge county (British: Rockbridge County) is a county located in the central part of United States of America Virginia. It was 22,307 people, and the population in the 2010 national census rose 7.2% from 20,808 people of 2000 [1]. County seat is the Lexington city which is an independent city (a population of 7,042 people [2]), but, with another independent city Buena Vista (a population of 6,650 people [3]), is not included in the population of the county. But the domain of the county contains both cities.
