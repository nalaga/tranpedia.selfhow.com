The actress that Kim ヘスク (a kanji: 金海淑, Hangul Alphabet :김해숙, Latin transliteration: on KIM Hae-sook, December 30, 1955 -) [1] is from Republic of Korea Busan Metropolitan City. I am active mainly on a movie and a TV drama.

Career

I learned from 慶熙大学校看護学科, but leave school in midcourse. I was chosen as the seventh open call for participants adoption talent of MBC in 1975 and made my debut in a TV drama "third classroom" of the broadcast in the same year. A movie appeared in "an apple to be able to be angry" at in 1976. There was the time when I closed entertainment activity, but I got married and "I looked and appeared in a sheep record" as the leading role in 1980 four years later and returned.

After having won Actress in a Supporting Role Award in KBS performance award in 2004 in 2000, I won a prize for prize best performance (actress) in "dear your parents" in 2005. A Corea drama Festival judge won a prize in Ogane Prize Actress in a Supporting Role Award and lesson day movie prize Actress in a Supporting Role Award with "femme F tar" particularly in "a wife club of the chaff and bran" in 2008. I appeared for vampire movie "thirst" in the part of mother of the friend of the chief character in 2009, the following day and won prize for guardian deity of the east in the shape of a dragon movie Actress in a Supporting Role Award.
