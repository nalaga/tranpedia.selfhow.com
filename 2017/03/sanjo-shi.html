Sanjo-shi (さんじょうし) is the city which is almost located in the central part of Niigata.

Summary

Throughout the prefecture eminent manufacturing town. Most of the city limits consist of the municipalities of former Minamikanbara-gun and are classified in the Nakagoshi district of Niigata, but may be included in the Kaetsu district to be seen in later car number plates. In late years a name of "県央地域" is commonly used, and it is in adjacent Tsubame-shi and metropolis of the area consisting of Kamo-shi, Tagami-machi, Yahiko-mura.

Current Sanjo-shi started with former Sanjo-shi and Sakae-cho, Minamikanbara-gun, the new merger by 3 cities, towns and villages of county Shimoda-mura (2005) on May 1 in 2005. The population at the time of the merger counted approximately 108,000 and became the city that had Niigata-shi, Nagaoka-shi, population of the prefecture fourth place next to Joetsu-shi. The recent population changes between the fifth place from the prefecture fourth place with Shibata-shi having the scale.
