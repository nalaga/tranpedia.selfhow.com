Kaikyo Line opened Honshu and Hokkaido as JR line to contact in substitution for Seikan route (Seikan ferry) navigated conventionally (1988) between Aomori Station - Hakodate Station on March 13 in 1988 after Japanese National Railways division and privatization.

A lot of trains which link Hokkaido to Honshu are operated. Kaikyo Line introduces digital ATC (DS-ATC) to the Hokkaido Shinkansen opening of business, too.
