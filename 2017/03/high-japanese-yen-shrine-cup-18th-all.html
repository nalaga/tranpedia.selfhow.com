The high Japanese yen shrine cup 18th all-Japan use (U-15) soccer championship (たかまどのみやはいだい 18 かいぜんにほん use (under 15) soccer せんしゅけんたいかい) is a meeting of 2006 of the high Japanese yen shrine cup all-Japan use (U-15) soccer championship.

It was held from December 10, 2006 through December 29, and a Gamba Osaka Jr. use served as first championship.

Competition method

  • Higher two teams of the group league go into the second round.
  • A group league, the second round is 40 minutes half together, and the game is held.
  • When there is not victory or defeat in the second round in 80 minutes, I perform the extra time of the half for ten minutes and decide a victory team by a penalty shoot-out when still I am not accompanied by an end.
