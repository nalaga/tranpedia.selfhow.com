Summary

Sony, Tokyu Corporation, three companies of Toyota Motor Corporation establish AII (A eye eye) plan Co., Ltd. for a plan of the contents distribution service (CDS) provider business in April, 2000 [1]. AII is an abbreviated designation of "Advanced Internet Integration". I laid emphasis on animation delivery as contents for broadbands from the beginning of establishment and performed a "TUBE Hawaii live" delivery proof experiment (targeted for approximately 80,000 households) to become the first Japanese trial on July 20, the same year.

I changed a company name of it to AII Co., Ltd. in December, 2000 and started content distribution business for broadbands in earnest [2].
