SRT (train)

In this item, I explain a Korean train. Please see "SRT" about other uses by "the Thai national railways" about the Thai railroad.

A bullet train name of SR company that SRT was introduced with the water Nishihira swamp Highway line (there was the tentative name called the metropolitan area high speed line at the time of the start of construction) opening of business in Korea in 2016 and the brand. I mean Super Rapid Train or SR Train.

I take route network (water west rapid transit railway, English: Suseo High Speed Railways, Korean :수서고속철도) to Pusan, Kinoura on the starting point at water west Station.
