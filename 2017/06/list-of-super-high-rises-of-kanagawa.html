A list of super high-rises of Kanagawa (かながわけんのちょうこうそうけんちくぶつのいちらん) is a list of super high-rises (in the laws and ordinances top of Japan more than 60m in height than (Building Standard Act Article 20 first [1])) more than 110m in height in Kanagawa, Japan.

Table of contents

General statement

The building which is the most expensive in Kanagawa is the Yokohama Landmark Tower in Yokohama-shi (295.8m in height, 70 stories).

The super high-rise of Kanagawa is built a lot around Musashikosugi in northern Kawasaki-shi with Yokohama-shi that is prefectural seat.
