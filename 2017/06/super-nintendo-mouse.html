Summary

It is the ball-type mouse of 2 buttons type and is available by a game of for exclusive use of the Super Nintendo mouse or the correspondence. I am connected to the controller connector of the front part of Super Nintendo body and use it. The model number is SNS-016. The mouse pad (SNS-017) made of plastic is bundled.

This mouse might let the main body break down when connected to the main body with a super multitap (machine enabling a play more than three people at the same time) that is a peripheral device of same Super Nintendo, and instructions of so are written in the instruction manual of the game software using the peripheral device concerned.

It was released by "Mario paint" and bundling at first, and the purchase in the simple substance was impossible, but even a simple substance was released later. It was the time when the software corresponding to the mouse gradually spread with a personal computer at the time of 1992, and this Super Nintendo mouse assumed simulation game correspondence, and the spread was expected. However, there was little correspondence software and did not come to greatly spread.
