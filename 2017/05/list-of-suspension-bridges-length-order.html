List of suspension bridges (length order)

World's best Akashi Kaikyo Bridge (Japan) of the maximum span length

A list of suspension bridges (the suspension bridge I am and am not scattered) is a list of by the maximum span long (distance between the main tower) order world suspension bridges.

When I arrange suspension bridges in length order, I generally employ the greatest span head. However, even full length may not be long even if a maximum span length is long.

The main span length is correlative with the height of the main tower and engineering and a design, architectural complexity.
