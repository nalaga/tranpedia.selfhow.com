The ninth air wing (the ninth こうくうだん, British designation: 9th Air Wing) is one of the military units mainly composed of the use of the fighter jet belonging to the Southwest Air Lines concoction group under the Air Self-Defense Force Airlines total unit servant. The headquarters is located in the Naha base (Naha-shi, Okinawa) and I carry out the anti-encroachment of air space measures for an approach, the unidentified plane invading mainly in the air space of the South-West Islands area and am in charge of the administrative task of the Naha base. There is the cognizance of the training air boundary in the Okinawa stationing United States Armed Forces, and a training schedule is set in weekly discussion, and it characterizes it that adjustment is always necessary and includes it.

I moved existing fighter corps one in our base, and, with a change of the security environment, two squadron organization and thing that I reorganized in "the ninth air wing" (tentative name) [1] from the 83rd flying corps of the forerunner after having done it were decided based on general rules of the defense plan shown cabinet decision on December 17, 2010 and new Chukibo [2].

It was listed in the Defense White Paper of the version in 2015 that I did the reorganization to the air wing with the end of the fiscal year. It is said, "acquisition, maintenance of the air control becoming the premise to realize effective restraint and coping are enabled" about the reinforcement of the squadron [3].

It was performed announcement [4] of [5] the reorganization to "the ninth air wing" being carried out after a cabinet meeting of January 26 on January 31, and a formation conclusion ceremony was carried out in Naha base on January 31.
