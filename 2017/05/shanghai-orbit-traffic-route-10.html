Shanghai orbit traffic Route 10 (the zhongwen notation which does not call Shanghai きどうこうつう 10 it: authority of Shanghai 轨 coming and going 10 线, English sentence notation: Shanghai Metro Line 10) is a pike of the Shanghai subway running through a mirage in the People's Republic of China. From rainbow bridge road Station to rainbow bridge 1 号航站楼駅, it is a part of 元々空港専用鉄道徐虹支線. Another name is called 徐虹線.

Summary

One of the wide route that crosses the Shanghai center from the northeast to the southwest, and constitutes "four length three wide part." It is an important route connecting Xuhui District and Hongkou District, and, in the northeast, the southwest is comprised of a branch line to way Station in navigating it from the main line binding rainbow Bridge Station Station that is terminal Station of 京滬高速鉄道 together via upper sea rainbow bridge International Airport and paths of dragon Valley Station from 新江湾城駅 of Yangpu District, and, 36km in total length, there are 31 stations in total. Construction was pushed forward to be inaugurated by Shanghai expo held in 2010, and part section was inaugurated. As for the opening to traffic to rainbow bridge Station Station, attendance was really started on November 30, 2010 [1] that planned October, 2010.

As the second construction, it is going to be extended northeast to the upper foreign countries Tamotsu Takahashi tax ward.

The orbit traffic route of Shanghai is right-hand traffic basically, but, in the case of the ride, attention is necessary because it is left-hand traffic only for section of 虹橋火車站 from rainbow bridge 1 号航站楼駅 of this route.
