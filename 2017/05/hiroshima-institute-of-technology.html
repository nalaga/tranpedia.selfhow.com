Hiroshima Institute of Technology (ひろしまこうぎょうだいがく, English: Hiroshima Institute of Technology) is a Japanese private university establishing the headquarters at 2-1-1, Miyake, Saeki-ku, Hiroshima-shi, Hiroshima. It was installed in 1963. As for the abbreviated designation of the university, the Hirocoh striding along is Hiroshima College of Engineering.
