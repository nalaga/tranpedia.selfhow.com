Dream (dream) is a Japanese group of 3 entertainment group for women. LDH position. A label is rhythm zone.

On January 1, 2000, I make my debut as dream. On June 25, 2007, I change my name in DRM (D are M). On July 22, 2008, I change my name to Dream, and the member individual switches to the alphabet notation of the name, too.
