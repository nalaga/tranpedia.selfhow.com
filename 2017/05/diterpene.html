Diterpene

Diterpene

Diterpene (Diterpene) is constructed by four isoprene and is a kind of terpene with a molecular formula of C20H32. It is derived than geranylgeranyl pyrophosphoric acid. Diterpene becomes the frame of compounds important biologically such as retinol, retinal, phytol. The antibacterial and anti-inflammatory nature is known.

Biosynthesis

All diterpene is made from produced geranylgeranyl pyrophosphoric acid by prenylation of farnesyl-pyrophosphate [1]; [2].
