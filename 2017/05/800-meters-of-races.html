800 meters of races (English: 800 metres) are classified in a middle range race in a track-and-field truck item to compete for thyme running 800 meters.

Summary

I do two laps of land trucks. A start runs in the separate zone just after that and becomes the open lane when I pass through the first lap second section.

In late years world top-level is under 13 seconds per 100m, and a tendency of becoming it is remarkable speed. There is the nickname called "a land martial art" after running at full speed at the speed that is almost such a sprint because a position fight or tactics after I enter the opening lane section are intense, and there are many accidents such as contact, the fall, and an attention degree and popularity are high in Europe. Cases abbreviated to 800m (800 meters) and 800 are often found among a competition and the people concerned. In addition, I may be called an 800 meters race by the athletic meets of a junior high school and the high school.

It cannot be said that all man and woman of them is high in the level of the Japanese player in the current world by the 800m race, but the first medal in the Japan female athlete of the Olympics is a silver medal of Kinue Hitomi who acquired it in this item in the Amsterdam Olympics in 1928. But the women's 800 meters was not accepted for an official record by Japanese land ream, and the record was not recognized officially at that time even if the results of Hitomi were recognized officially. It is from 1929 that an official record of the women's 800 meters was recognized officially [1].
