Dai-3-ku, Fukushima (multiseat constituency)

Dai-3-ku, Fukushima (the third く which prostrates itself, and does not lose) is the electoral district which assumes whole current Fukushima Beach street an area in an electoral district of the House of Representatives which ever existed. The fixed number at the time of the abolition is 3. When I shifted to single-member electorate system, I am divided and part from a part of one ward of Fukushima now in five wards of Fukushima.
