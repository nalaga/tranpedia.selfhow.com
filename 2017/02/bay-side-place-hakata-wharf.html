Bay side place Hakata Wharf (bay side place はかたふとう, a British designation: Bay side place Hakata wharf) is a compound facility at Hakata-ku, Fukuoka-shi, Fukuoka Hakata Wharf.

Summary

There are plural wharfs in the Hakata Port, and one of those is Hakata Wharf. Two passenger terminals are at Hakata Wharf, and the building where the tenant including an eating out shop to provide along with the first terminal, the general shop enters is bay side place Hakata. I consist of three ridges of A ridge close against Hakata Wharf police box, central B ridge, the C ridge leading the first terminal. Generally, as well as the first terminal and the building of the tenant, I call all the facilities at Hakata Wharf including the Hakata port tower collectively with "bay side place". I speak the bay side place of this wide sense in this clause.

The facilities create an atmosphere of the waterfront. The general producer is Yasuhiro Hamano. It is a design making Fisherman's Wharf of San Francisco closely resembling.

I open in 1991. The management mother's body was sun peer Hakata of the third sector where Fukuoka-shi finances, but is bankrupted in September, 2005 (later description). As for the management administration, it is shifted to the Kyudenko net produce of the Kyudenko subsidiary later, and business is continued now by bay side place Hakata Co., Ltd.

Issue of bankruptcy

The bay side place was planned as part of Hakata harbor redevelopment. "Sun peer Hakata" is established for capital 400 million yen other than 5% of Fukuoka-shi, Hakatako Kaihatsu (third sector) 10%, Fukuoka Corporation 10%, West Japan real-estate development 10%, Fukuoka land 10% in 1988 by the investments of the local company such as Kyushu Electric Power, and bay side place Hakata opens it in June, 1991. Each generation Fukuoka-shi staff alumnus acted as the president and was a pulling in customers facility of the city eminence more than 4 million number of the annual visitors at the time of the opening of business.

However, for Seaside Momochi and city large size commercial facilities and a factor such as competing, and visitors decreasing, I continued it until period in March, 2000, and, in addition to the badness of the traffic access, it was deficit operation. In June, 2005 of the thing that large warm bath facilities "Hakata first bath" or restaurant "Hakata stand-cho" start a business, and the number of the visitors restores 3 million lay figures a year which bay side city Co., Ltd. runs in November, 2002, the company applies for voluntary bankruptcy from influence by the rapid diversification [4]. The store which the company ran in bay side place did it on all five of 25 stores, floor space and occupied a quarter and, in result September, 2005 when I suffered a management blow for sun peer Hakata that relied on 70% of the income source to a tenant income, I applied to the Fukuoka District Court for an application of Civil Rehabilitation Law and was accepted. As for the aggregate amount of indebtedness, financing 340 million yen from Fukuoka-shi became the debt waiver for approximately 3,900 million yen (beat as for the finance debt 3,300 million yen). In addition, it became the first failure in the third sector where Fukuoka-shi financed, but, as for this failure, even the criticism to Fukuoka Olympics design was mentioned, and it was one connected in Yamasaki municipal administration nonconfidence.

The assets of sun peer Hakata are sold to Kyudenko and, after failure, will be affected by the rearranging of the debt. Kyudenko planned to run commercial amusement facilities in cooperation with the Mori Building city plan of the subsidiary of Mori Building dealing with Roppongi Hills, but comes to a deadlock. It reopens a market and a restaurant handling a local farm and marine industries thing as the core afterwards in March, 2010 [5]. This revival plan would increase commercial broadcasting FM radio station, cross fm under reconstruction and I abolished the Tenjin glitter just what studio which I put it together for resumption and established on the Iwataya Department Store Tenjin head office new building the first floor and transferred it in bay side place. "The bay side place Hakata cooperation society" by local powerful companies was established to push forward use promotion of the bay side place and the maintenance of neighboring areas in July, 2010 [6].
