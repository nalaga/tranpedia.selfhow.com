Helmet (motorcycle)

In the case of a fall and an accident taking, the helmet is one of the physical equipment to protect the head for crew in a motorcycle and a vehicle to follow. The thing due to the head injury is the most, and, as for the cause of death in the accident during a motorcycle ride, the wearing of the helmet is done with recommendation to duty as the thing which therefore prevents a head injury, and is reduced.

Origin

A motorcycle is invented in the late 19th century, and use began in the early 20th century, but there is not the custom to wear a helmet for a long time; for the accident have not been studied. However, I got the conclusion that it largely reduced the death toll to that Hugh Cairns of the surgeon who was the member of the treatment team used a helmet including the study of the injury by getting on at motorcycle accidents because Thomas Edward Lawrence known as Arabian Lawrence was in an accident during a motorcycle ride and died of a head injury. This is an immediate early study indicating the effectiveness of the helmet [1]. The increase of the fatal accident was brought into question while motorcycles spread in all the countries of the world and came to be become standardization, duty afterwards in each country.

Difference by the shape

 
Helmet (from the left sequentially open face form, full-faced helmet form, Flip Up form) for the motorcycle

The classifications by the shape are as follows.

Half form
I am called the half sphere, half cap, wooden bowl form. Put it on willingly, and it is an advantage to be cheap, but it is a fault that a protection range is small, and a shock to the both sides of the head region is easy to become the fatal injury in particular (the skull of the part equal to the upper part of the ear is relatively thin). I am prepared by the standard for the motorcycle to 125cc cubic centimeter displacement in Japan.
Three-quarters form
A protection range more temporal than half form is enlarged to degree to cover up half of the ears and is called the cicada jet form from the place that is half form and a middle shape of the open face form (jet form). There is much use by the motorcycle for duties. There is the thing which a shield can attach and might injure a face when I fall down from face if there is not a shield. But even if there is a shield, I can protect face, but might damage a chin. Things prepared by the standard for the motorcycle to 125cc cubic centimeter displacement are often found in Japan.
Open face form
The type that I cover both sides of the head part and almost all occipital, but the face is exposed. I am called the jet form from the shape of the helmet which the pilot of the old fighter jet put on. It is safer than two mentioned above and does not spoil the area of the field of vision, a feeling of opening, convenience (because I do not cover the face, I drink water with putting on a helmet and can talk). There is the thing which a shield can attach. I am almost prepared by the standard for the motorcycle of all displacement volume in Japan.
Full-faced helmet form
I am called the integral form. I added Japanese spaniel guard (cover of the part of the chin) to open face form. The view will be covered other than a part to secure. It is the higher safe (when I fall down from face) biggest advantage and is particularly superior in comfort in running it at high speed because it is hard to roll up wind again. I can prevent the damage to face and the head, but it is easy to take load to a neck and might cause spinal cord injury or dyspnea. In addition, because lips are covered, I may disturb lifesaving measures such as the securing of respiratory tract, and therefore there is the thing of the structure that can take off a helmet at the time of the accident outbreak called the helmet remover immediately. A helmet with a built-in air bag is developed by the APC systems that is a Spanish helmet maker. Furthermore, the safety only costs an air bag jacket or a neck brace system and a thing putting together to reduce the load to a neck. I am prepared by the standard for the motorcycle of all displacement volume basically. I develop the helmet that Bikesystems, BMW embed HUD including SKULLY.

With full-faced helmet form and the open face form, there is the thing that the air port called the ventilation system is established and becomes comfortable during a run against an appearance by such a type coolly. I make a UV cut on a shield for a woman, and there is the thing of the structure that reduces involution of the wind of the side shield, and made much of lightness and is called family form recently.

The half form seems to be cool at a glance, but there is not a ventilation system, and the inside is stuffy in the summertime because there is not breathability and becomes cold so that a face is numb with the numerousness of the outcrop in winter. It is more unpleasant than jet form and full-faced helmet form at such a point. In addition, there is the problem to become hard to hear neighboring sounds because of the noise of the round tile sound at the time of the high-speed run because the half form exposes an ear to light.

Because the full-faced helmet form made exclusively for off-road is made as the race use that I did including motocross, the view is bad. Therefore I take off a shield and may wear goggles. Every shield can open and close a face guard part while being full-faced helmet form, and there is the thing of the structure that the division, the union with the hat body is free and is called Flip Up form (system form) recently. The crossover form that a no runs group produces can attach and detach Japanese spaniel guard depending on the situation and can change the visor, too.

Test method

There are a common part and an original part by each maker and standard about the test method in the safety of the helmet of the motorcycle and is a representative test as follows.

Shock absorption test
人体頭部模型（ダミーヘッド。以下、人頭模型）にヘルメットをかぶらせ、各規格で定められた高さから落とすことにより人頭模型へのダメージを検証するテスト。規格によって落とす高さやどこにダメージを与えるかが違う。
Snell M2010では7.75 m/s (27.9km/h) での衝突を行う[13]。
耐貫通性テスト
突起物に対するヘルメットの強度を測るテスト。実際に重量物をヘルメットに落として行う。路上には突起物が無数にあるためそれがヘルメットに当たった際にヘルメットを貫通してしまうようでは安全とはいえないことから行われる。
顎紐テスト
転倒時や事故発生時に顎紐が伸びたり、切れたりすることでヘルメットが頭から外れてしまわないかを調べるテスト。顎紐に重量物を取り付けて規定の距離で落として顎紐の状態を調べる。
ロールオフテスト
ヘルメットを脱がすような力を加え、ヘルメットが脱げないかを調べるテスト。壁に固定した人頭模型にヘルメットをかぶせ、ヘルメットが脱げるような力を加えて調べる。
チンバーテスト
ヘルメットの顎（チン・バー）部分の強度を測るテスト。転倒時に顎を打つライダーも多いため顔面を傷つける恐れがあることから顎部分に錘を落とすことで強度を調べる。
