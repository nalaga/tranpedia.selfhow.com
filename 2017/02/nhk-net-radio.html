NHK net radio らじる ★ らじる

NHK net radio "らじる ★ らじる" (N H Kay net radio らじるらじる) is an official name, a nickname of the IP simultaneous radio simultaneous delivery service (live streaming) of the NHK radio of the Japanese Broadcasting Corporation (NHK).

Summary

In among the mountains or a concrete building, I deliver it on the Internet as complimentary measures to improve the hearing situation in a difficulty hearing area receiving the interference of the electric wave from a place and the foreign country where an electric wave is hard to reach. An object is radio primary radio second FM. As for the service name, an official name is NHK net radio, nickname がらじる ★ らじる. The commercial broadcasting radio station started simultaneous delivery service radiko by similar Internet on March 15 (2010) in 2010.
