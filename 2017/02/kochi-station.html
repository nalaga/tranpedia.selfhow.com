Kochi Station (Chie comes in this way) is a Shikoku Railway Co. (Shikoku Railway Company) Dosan Line station at 2, Sakaedacho, Kouchi-shi, Kochi. The comment of the station guidance panel "a station of Sunday City when Ryoma and a good point are thick." The station number is D45, K00. The Dosan Line station number is divided into D (Awaikeda, Kotohira, the Tadotsu area) and K (the Susaki area) in the starting point at Kochi Station.

Other than a train of Dosan Line, the train of the Tosa Kuroshio Current railroad Asa line (a nickname: noodles, なはり line) running in it from Gomen Station arrives and departs at our station.
