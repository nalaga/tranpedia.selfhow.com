The volleyball world championship (English: 2006 FIVB VOLLEYBALL WORLD CHAMPIONSHIPS JAPAN) is the volleyball world championship held in Japan in 2006 for 2,006 years (as for the boy as for the 16th, the girl the 15th).

A girl meeting was started on November 17, 2006 a boy meeting from October 31, 2006 to November 16 until December 3.

Game form

1st Round(STAGE 1)

  • At first 24 teams are divided into four groups (pool A, B, C, D) by six teams and perform one round of round-robin.
  • It is 16 countries of four countries from each group to send to the next round.

2nd Round(STAGE 2)

  • I divide 16 countries into two groups and carry out one round of round-robin here.
    • As for the grouping, higher four countries of the group of A and D, pool F are high rank four countries of the group of B and C pool E.
    • But it is four games that really play a game that the results of three games of the country which fought in a primary qualifier just carry it over and become (results joint ownership does not fight in the second qualifier).
  • It is 12 countries of six countries from each group to send to the next round.

Final Round(STAGE 3)

  • 12 countries are divided into three tournament groups by the order and decide order.
    • I am greatly shared with a 1-4 rank jump-off (by higher two countries of the second league), 5-8 rank jump-off (by two countries of the 3.4 rank), 9-12 rank jump-off (by two countries of the 5.6 rank), and semifinals = spare war and final = order jump-off are performed each.
  • The country which won in the final of the 1-4 rank jump-off is the number one in the world.
