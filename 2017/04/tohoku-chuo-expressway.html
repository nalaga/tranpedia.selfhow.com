Tohoku Chuo Expressway (とうほくちゅうおうじどうしゃどう, TOHOKU-CHUO EXPRESSWAY) is an expressway of approximately 268km in total length to connect Soma-shi, Fukushima to Akita Expressway in Yokote-shi, Akita via Fukushima-shi, Yonezawa-shi, Yamagata, Yamagata-shi, Shinjo-shi in the starting point. The abbreviated designation Tohoku Chuo Expressway (とうほくちゅうおうどう, TOHOKU-CHUO EXPWY).

E13 is allotted to the route number by the expressway numbering [1].
