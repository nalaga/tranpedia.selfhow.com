Hanyu Station (はにゅうえき) is a TOBU Railway, Chichibu Railway station at 1, Minami, Hanyu-shi, Saitama.

Isesaki Line of TOBU Railway and Chichibu Main Line (Chichibu Line) of Chichibu Railway run in it and become the junction station.
