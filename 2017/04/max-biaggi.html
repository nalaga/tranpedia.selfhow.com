Max Biaggi [1] (Massimiliano "Max" Biaggi, June 26, 1971 -) is a former motorcycle racer of Italy Rome native place [2] [3].

I got a champion for 4 consecutive years from 1994 through 1997 in the road race world championship 250cc class and recorded the annual ranking second place three times in highest peak 500cc class, the MotoGP class. I move the place of the activity for the super motorcycle world championship from 2007 and shine to the Italian first champion by the championship in 2010 [4]. I got the second world champion by the championship in 2012 and expressed a retirement afterwards.

The nickname "yl コルサーロ" (pirates) "caesars."
