Yoshiaki Kato (father Yoshiaki and others [2] / Yoshiaki [3]) is a military commander, a daimyo from to the Edo era from the Azuchimomoyama era. In raising from an early age a large number of people of Hideyoshi Toyotomi, it is one of seven spears, seven commanders of the Mt. 賤 ヶ. Matsuyama, Iyo feudal clan and Michinoku Aizu feudal clan first generation feudal lord.

The popular name changes grandchild six, left horse assistant と later same as office and rank. Yoshiaki of 諱 introduced itself as 茂勝 (繁勝) by later self-introduction at the start, too.

Life

Hideyoshi Era

I was born as the eldest son of Noriaki Kato (Sannojo Kishi Noriaki) who was a vassal of Matsudaira Ieyasu [1] in (1563), 三河国幡豆郡永良郷 [4] for Eiroku era six years. Because Mikawa of the born age was a riot totally, and I did a group, and father was defeated by the riot group against Ieyasu totally, and it was the wandering body, Yoshiaki roamed, too.

Father served Nagahama lord of a castle, Hideyoshi Hashiba and received 300 stones after the vicissitudes to Oomi country and lived in the Yajima volost. Yoshiaki helped with the peddling of the horse, but I was found in Kato Jingtai (father of Mitsuyasu Kato) that there was superior nature in spite of being childhood and was recommended as Hideyoshi. Even if I came to give Kato family name taking advantage of this as an adopted child of Jingtai, Hideyoshi says this [1].

Hideyoshi let Yoshiaki serve it as a page of adopted child Hidekatsu Hashiba. However, Yoshiaki was embedded with Hidekatsu in Harima attack of (1576) without permission for Tensho era four years. It is found out and is flown into a rage with the Mrs. Hideyoshi, おねの where this is a step mother of Hidekatsu that a page is going to accomplish a first campaign before the lord before the first campaign what it is. The おねの person appealed to drive out it immediately saying that Yoshiaki was "a person of the scoundrels", but Hideyoshi evaluated will of Yoshiaki adversely and decided not only to have held it, but also gave the in camp the provisions of 300 stones, and to do it with 直臣.

I participated in Miki Castle siege (1578) according to the Hideyoshi forces in March for Tensho era six years and gave glory to a first campaign and raised two severed heads only after I did it to 15 years old by the fight of the detailed Hisashi Nakazu hair mountain in April. （Miki battle）　

When Nobunaga Oda fell it by (1582), Honnoji-Temple Incident for Tensho era ten years, the military of Hideyoshi who came back in middle National University ebb increased Yoshiaki just before a fight of Yamasaki on June 13. I am embedded with (1584), a fight and a Saiga attack of Komaki, Nagakute for Tensho era 12 years.
