The bell cold type patrol boat (I make bell cold order English: Suzukaze-class patrol craft) is ship's class of the patrol boat of the Coast Guard. The division top a CL type, the official name model of a vessel 20 meters type [2]. In addition, is called the ではひめぎく type now because a name ship changed its name with an assignment substitute [3]; [4].

Origin

In the Coast Guard, it started the maintenance of domestic round of inspection vessels and boats at a plan in 1949, but it was 15 meters type CL I cut the top, and to have been placed on duty (そよかぜ type). Because a limit was given to performance and the quantity of of the patrol boat possession as for this in Kaiho foundation those days, I put it outside the limit and was designed as a port boat to supplement a patrol boat in the limit shore sea area around the base, but because it was very excellent and got the trust of the crew, I continued being constructed only by a minor change without hardly changing outline placement [2] and hung the last batch にあたるちよかぜ type in 50 from 1968, and 96 were constructed really [3].

However, a displacement increased the 15 meters type by accumulation of longtime equipment reinforcement, and the parts which touched the rule with the revision of ship security law relations rules increased. From this, I changed the hull dimensions with (mountain lily type) for building after 1977 for the first time and planned upsizing and was called 18 meters type in the department. And it will be started charges ship building of the ちよかぜ type by the plan in 1991, but this will plan upsizing more. This is this model [2]. In addition, after ちよかぜ-shaped military retirement completion, building wants to be continued for update use of the Hayama lily type this time [3]; [4].

Design

With this model, I plan the improvement of the speed in response to the speedup of a pleasure boat and the hooker. I made a model of a vessel have good run performance and planned weight reduction as a product made in aluminum alloy with a superstructure and equipment of a ship product [2]. The main hull is high tension steel like .18 meters of conventional 15 meters types type [3]; [4]. A wheelhouse, a township is enlarged with upsizing, and duties processing and improvement of the livability are planned. The chair with the damper for all crews is located in the wheelhouse, and there is the sofa of the bed combined use [5], and the first simple shower facilities are comprised in the township as a patrol boat again [2].

The main engine was strengthened for speedup, too, and a V-twelve 4 cycles diesel engine was equipped with. The main engine has MTU type and MAN type, and an MTU 12V183 TE92 type (910 ps / 2,230 rpm) is adopted by MAN D2842LYE[5], the MTU type by the MAN type [6].

In addition, a large number of subtypes exist because a majority was extremely constructed as for this model. A difference of the basic design level almost includes 4 subtypes such as follows.

Guard functional enhancement type
A building boat after the 2001 first revision plan [7]. I strengthened the elasticity-proof with [1] to strengthen the main plane output to 2,040HP. Because I abolish the drainage gun of the bow deck in compensation for weight increase, I am equipped with a petrol pump and a desorption-type drainage nozzle instead.

In addition, about the navigation area, I was considered to be the limited shore at first besides, but was changed by boat after "when cold" (CL-94) of the revision plan with effect of the United Nations Convention about the law of the sea in the sea near the shore in 1996. In addition, when include it to an equipment of a ship product, each may be classified in a north type and a south type because strengthen heating to an air conditioner depending on deployment, and the boat after the revision plan is equipped with a monitor by night on the wheelhouse in 2005 [3]; [4].

In addition, two 20 meters type Lighthouse watchman ships (hide a Hikari type) which I put appointed equipment of a ship for based on this model were constructed by the revision plan in the first in 1998, but they receive demand for vessels and boats increase for the guard, the rescue and are changed classification on a patrol boat on April 1, 2008 and are placed on duty as a しらうめ type patrol boat again. The main essential points are approximately equal to a book-shaped wide model, but there is a difference in the details including the position of the fender bar. In addition, "I win" (MS-03) is constructed as a radioactivity investigation boat to inspect radiation leaks at the time of a nuclear-powered ship call at a port, but the guard rescue like this model works other than a radioactivity investigation because this was equipped with radioactivity measuring equipment in a book-shaped Asanami type and is equal to this for the performance at all.
