With the early vote system (きじつぜんとうひょうせいど), it is one of the preliminary election systems in election of Japan or the referendum. A system established in 2 (2003) from December 1 in 2003 of Public Officers Election Act Article 48 and the system that was established in law Article 60 about the revised procedure of the Constitution of Japan.
