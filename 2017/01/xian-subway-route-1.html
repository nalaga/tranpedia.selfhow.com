Summary

By the route that ran through Xi'an City to the east and west, I made a trial on September 15, 2013 and was inaugurated [1]. In "Xi'an City castle town high speed orbit traffic line network plan" [authority of Xi'an City castle town high speed 轨 coming and going-based 设规划] of 2004, Route 1, the important main line of the north and south were done with Route 2 East-West important main lines. And, as an important route linking Xi'an north Station, an administrative central station, three development wards, Route 2 will be built earlier [2].
