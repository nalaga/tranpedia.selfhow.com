ギターロ (USS Guitarro, SS-363) is a submarine of U.S. Navy. One ship of the gateau grade submarine. The name of a warship means "a guitar" in Spanish and is associated with Rhinobatos schlegeli ギターロ (English version) of ray eyes with fame of Guitarfish.
