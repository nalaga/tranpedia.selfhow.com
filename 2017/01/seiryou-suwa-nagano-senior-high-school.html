Seiryou, Suwa, Nagano Senior High School, the affiliated junior high school (ながのけんすわせいりょうこうとうがっこう, ふぞくちゅうがっこう) is a public high school, junior high school at 1, Shimizu, Suwa-shi, Nagano.

Summary

I assume 諏訪郡立実科中学校 (old system) founded in (1895) in 1895 a forerunner. "Seiryou" of the school name comes from "Shimizugaoka" that is the place name.

At time of the Taisho democracy, words "自反而縮雖千萬人吾往矣" (the meaning that I will go ahead through one's way to so that I look back toward the act of oneself, and there are 10 million enemies みづからかへりみてなをくんばせんまんにんといへどもわれゆかむ if that it is right) of the Mencius "noble descendant Ox" which a student's union (student council saying in the public) wrote down on the flag becomes the school motto. In addition, a "self-government" "simple and sturdy" "hard work effort" of the school tradition is based.

A uniform and the school regulations do not exist and do it on the basis of the self-government by the student. Therefore a school festival (Seiryou festival) and an event, the club activities are run under the student's union, and there is little interference by the teacher.

It travels the classroom every class like a university. Each student confirms the communication matter to see a bulletin board. In addition, the campus does not have a clock.
