The route of the Nagoya municipal subway where Sakuradori Line (I boil it according to the cherry tree) links Tokushige Station of city Midori-ku from Nakamura-Kuyakusho Station of Nakamura-ku, Nagoya-shi, Aichi. The official name is Nagoya-shi high-speed railroad Route 6. The line color is red (■).

History

It is a planned route in council for urban traffic report 14th of (1972) for 1,972 years. By Higashiyama Line, wicket closing at Nagoya Station becomes common in those days in the commuter rush hour; at the mid-50s of the Showa era between Nagoya Station - Fushimi Station congestion rate more than 250% it was in a situation. Therefore at first construction between Nakamura-Kuyakusho Station - Imaike Station was carried out and, for congestion reducing of Higashiyama Line, practiced medicine (1989) as a bypass route on September 10 in 1989 [7]. The construction of the section built the structure to pass through the basement of Nagoya Station, and an adopted large-scale underpinning method of construction won engineering works prize from an academic society technique prize. The plate was buried in our line Nagoya Station home, but was removed in 2015.

Sakuradori Line assumed all homes an island expression assuming performing autocrat driving from the first and maintained the driver's cab of all vehicles on the line right side where a home was near. I started autocratic driving on February 16 (1994) in 1994 when introduction of the ATO was completed although a conductor boarded it at the time of the opening of business. On March 30 of the same age, Imaike Station - Nonami Station interval started a business.

Nonami Station - Tokushige Station interval started a business (2011) on March 27 in 2011 [8]. Because the opening of business became just after the 2011 off the Pacific coast of Tohoku Earthquake, I canceled a commemorative event and performed only the sale of commemorative goods such as D'ni Chieko ticket, the B train show tea. About the section, Nagoya-shi decided to build it as a next maintenance section (2000) in December 2000, and acquired the first-class railway business permission of the section (2003) on September 5 in 2003, and started construction [9]; [10]. The trial run with the opening of business used normal passenger service from January 22, 2011 and I assumed service after the opening to traffic and, before and after the passenger getting on and off at Nonami Station, performed it at Nonami Station - Tokushige Station.

In addition, about extension construction of Nonami Station - Tokushige Station, major general contractor caught the house investigation of the Nagoya District Public Prosecutors Office special investigation department and the Fair Trade Commission on the suspicion of collusion by a bid with the construction (2007) in January in 2007. The executives of major general contractor five companies were arrested for Antitrust Law alleged violation on February 28, the same year.
