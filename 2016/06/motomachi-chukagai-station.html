Motomachi-Chukagai Station (I wait for the cause ちゅうかがいえき) is a Yokohama rapid transit railway Minatomirai Line station in Yamashitacho, Naka-ku, Yokohama-shi, Kanagawa. I practiced medicine with the opening to traffic of Minatomirai Line on February 1, 2004. The station number is MM06.

I acquire a vice-name formally and refer it to Motomachi, a Chinatown (Yamashita Park) station (I wait for the cause and obtain it ちゅうかがい 〈 〉 that I cannot ask that I let you stop and come), but am generally called "Motomachi-Chukagai Station".

The around our station district (Kannai district) is appointed in the one "Yokohama downtown area" of the downtown area (twin core) in Yokohama-shi [2].

Origin of the station name

The tentative name at the plan stage was "Motomachi Station" that took the place name near the station location. However, I requested it to add "a Chinatown" than a person concerned with Yokohama Chinatown, and to do it at "Motomachi Chinatown Station", but a person concerned with Motomachi mall objected for this saying "do not do it with a Chinatown", and it was a debate on the Motomachi mall side and the Yokohama Chinatown side under the surface of the water. The station name would write both names in parallel at the top using "・" between Motomachi and a Chinatown, and the well- known "Yamashita park" as the sightseeing spot increased as a vice-name more after all.
