Kinzo Shin (しんきんぞう, from July 9, 1910 to December 26, 1988) is a Japanese actor. The real name: Sincerity Kinzo. I played an active part in the stage name of sincerity Kinzo from the mid-1960s through mid-1970s. Tomomi Tsutsui of a scriptwriter, the novelist is a niece. The cemetery is Yanaka Cemetery.

I am dismissed from an actor seat with a wife in 1955 and join the citizen of theatrical company art which Uno and others founded. I belong to the actor seat again though I am dismissed from people art in 1966 and play an active part as a freelance afterward.
