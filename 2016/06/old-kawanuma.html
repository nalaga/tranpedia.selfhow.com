The lagoon where Old Kawanuma (ふるかわぬま) was in Rikuzentakata-shi, Iwate. It was the greatest natural wetlands in Iwate [1], but most of the sandbar which separated Gulf of Hirota and Old Kawanuma after a massive tsunami caused by the 2011 off the Pacific coast of Tohoku Earthquake generated (2011) on March 11 in 2011 become extinct, and it is under the present conditions in the sea [3].
