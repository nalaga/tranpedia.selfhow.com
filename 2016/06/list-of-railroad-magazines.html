List of railroad magazines

A list of railroad magazines (てつどうざっしのいちらん) is a list of magazines (periodical literature) about the railroad. The things about the industry, the history, the model (railroad model) are listed. I do not Japanize it about the thing without the article in a Japanese edition about the name of magazine. Wikipedia Japanese edition did not have an article and linked to an other languages version when it was for other languages.
