John play Ford (John Playford, 1623 ノリッチ -1686 age London) is an English score publisher, bookseller.

Life

Until 1639 or 1640 through 1647 publisher John Benson (en:John Benson (publisher)) After having served probation under の, I opened the shop in the porch of the temple church. I publish "a dancing master" leaving the name of play Ford as a bookseller and the publisher in now in 1651.

Play Ford retired in 1684, and Henry play Ford (Henry Playford) of the son inherited the back.

In 1686, I die. Henry Purcell composed "elegy (Pastoral Elegy on the Death of Mr. John Playford) to send to death of John play Ford" in mourning death of play Ford.
