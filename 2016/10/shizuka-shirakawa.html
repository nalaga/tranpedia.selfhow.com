Shizuka Shirakawa (しらかわしずか, from April 9, 1910 to October 30, 2006) is a scholar of Japanese classical Chinese, an Orientalist. The degree Doctorate in Literature (Kyoto University). Professor emeritus at Ritsumeikan University, a honorary hall friend. FukuiFukui-shiA native place.
