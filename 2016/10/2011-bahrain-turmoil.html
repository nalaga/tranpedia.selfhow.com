Anti-government demonstration

It was the first movement this time in the Gulf States blessed with oil resources [2]. It is reported afterwards by Al Jazeera that anti-government demonstration is planned on February 14 [3]. February 14 was chosen as a day in commemoration of the tenth anniversary when a Bahraini nation action charter draft was adopted by a referendum [4]. In the background of the demonstration, there is opposition of the Shiah occupying the Islam Sunni of the person of royal families Harry F to hold national power and the majority of the nation. The Shiah receives finding employment discrimination, and Shiah inhabitants are the main constituents, and this demonstrator demands job creation and the raise in wage, the retreat of the Harry F prime minister for holding the office 40 years, introduction of the parliamentary cabinet system for the government, too [5]; [6].
