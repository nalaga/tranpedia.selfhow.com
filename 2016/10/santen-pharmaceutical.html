SANTEN PHARMACEUTICAL Co., Ltd. (さんてんせいやく, Santen Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd.) is a pharmaceutical company of Japan.

It produces the eyewash of the lowest common denominator for ophthalmology, ophthalmology medicine, eye drops and sells it mainly. Part of Tokyo Stock Exchange presentation (securities cord: 4536).

The catch copy "person, eyes, healthy" (English :A Clear Vision For Life).
