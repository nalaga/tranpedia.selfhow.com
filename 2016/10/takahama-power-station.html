Takahama power station (たかはまはつでんしょ) is a nuclear power plant of Kansai Electric Power in Takahama-cho, Ooi-gun, Fukui. With the Takahama Nuclear Power Plant (たかはまげんしりょくはつでんしょ), Takahama Nuclear Power Plant (たかはまげんぱつ).
