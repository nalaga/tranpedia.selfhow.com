The Masada soccer ground (まさだ soccer じょう) is a gridiron for exclusive use of the soccer in Masuda, Higashi-ku, Okayama-shi, Okayama.

F diArno Okayama belonging to J League uses it as a practice field. Okayama-shi owns the facilities and F diArno Okayama gym Co., Ltd. runs it as a designated manager and manages it.
