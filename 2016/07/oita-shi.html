Summary

Oita-shi is one of the major cities of East Kyushu that is almost located in the center of the Oita shore part. I have a population of approximately 480,000 and receive designation of the core city. It is the first place city where approximately 40% of population in Oita are concentrated and is the center of the Oita urban area of approximately 700,000 population. The current city limits have ever belonged to Oita-gun and Kitaamabe-gun (until the early period of Meiji Kaifu-gun).

It was old, and Kokufu of the Bungo country was put and was called in the prefecture (there is a crucian carp). It developed as a castle town of Otomo in the Middle Ages and became the center of the Christianity propagation in cause, Japan of the protection of Christian daimyo Sorin Otomo for the age of civil strife, and European medieval culture opened a flower.

I grow rapidly with the advance of heavy and chemical industries such as the steel industry, the chemical industry as a manufacturing town for the rapid economic growth period, and in late years a location of the electronics industry advances.
