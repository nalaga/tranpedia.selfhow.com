Shinonome (しののめ) a destroyer [1] of the Japanese Navy [2]. The sixth warship [3] of 特型 (first-class destroyer snowstorm type). The original name of a warship a 40th destroyer [1]. One ship [4] of the classification of clouds (spider class). For vessels of the Japanese Navy with this name is the second ship following Shinonome model destroyer "Shinonome".
