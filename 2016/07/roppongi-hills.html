Home generation of electricity

In Roppongi Hills, Roppongi energy service performs all by electricity and supply of the thermal power including the gas. Roppongi energy service is a specific electricity company established by Mori Building and Tokyo Gas [8].

I adopt a gas turbine cogeneration and establish facilities's original energy plant as fuel with city gas and supply the electricity for several thousand households of standard home within a level. Power supply is in this way enabled without receiving most of the electricity restrictions such as blackouts [9]. When supply of the gas stops, I purchase power from Tokyo Electric Power and take the double backup system generating electricity with stored kerosene by any chance when it becomes impossible. This depends on the request of the foreign-affiliated tenant for the office environment where power supply does not settle on at the time of the emergency either [10].

It did not operate in the normal, but the power supply function did not remain in the building street and was done for the outside and supplied surplus electricity to the outside by the power saving of the building street in an occasion of the electricity shortage with the 2011 off the Pacific coast of Tohoku Earthquake (the Great East Japan Earthquake) although being restrictive [11].
