The national high school soccer championship Shizuoka meeting (ぜんこくこうとうがっこう soccer せんしゅけんたいかいしずおかけんたいかい) is a Shizuoka qualifier meet of the national high school soccer championship.

Table of contents

Summary

  • Sponsorship: (one fortune) Shizuoka Football Association, federation of Shizuoka Senior High School physical education, Shizuoka Daiichi Television, Shizuoka Shimbun-sha

Game method

  • A primary tournament participating school (in 130 2007) plays a game by a tournament method and decides 16 second league participation schools. (September)
  • 16 schools which won and advanced to the next around of the second league first tournament are divided into 4 blocks and perform a league match of four opposition. (October)
  • Two high ranks of each second round second league block and special seed (the high school where candidate U-18 representative from Japan appointed from Football Association of Japan belongs to.in 2007 in three) play a game by a tournament method for national convention participation. (November)

Past representative school

  • The first - eighth makes the participation school from Shizuoka
  • The ninth - 17th is Tokai area meeting
  • The 18th - 22nd is a mountain god static meeting
  • The 26th - 30th is central part area meeting
  • The 31st - 32nd is Koshin static meeting
  • The 33rd - 34th is Tokai area meeting
  • It becomes the Shizuoka meeting after the 35th.
  • The 45th - 49th did not perform a qualifier and decided a participation school based on the national high school entirety, the results of the national polity by selection society.
