The State of Odessa (Ukrainian: Одеськаобласть オデースィカ オーブラスチ) is one of the states of Ukraine. The state office location is Odessa. President of former Georgia ミヘイル サアカシュヴィリ acts as the governor from 2015.

The State of Odessa is located in the river mouth zone of southwestern part of Ukraine, south Buch River, Denis's Teru River, the Danube. In the north, in the east, in the ムィコラーイウ state, the west, Moldova, the southwest contact with Romania ヴィーンヌィツャ state and キロヴォフラード state, and the south faces the Black Sea. A grassy plain accounts for the area of the State of Odessa mainly.

In the northern part of the State of Odessa, in the ポドィーリャ highlands, the southern part engraved into the deep gorge, the level ground with a pond and the lake spreads out. In the river of more than 200 to flow into the Black Sea in a place of State of Odessa, a flow; inner; the biggest thing is the Danube, D'ni Teru river, コードィマ river and サウランカ river.

The climate is a continental climate. As for the mean temperature of January, -3.5 degrees С, the mean temperature of July become 22 degrees С.
