Summary

"Departmental order (Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare law 111) to revise a part about payment, geriatric medicine and the request of the expense about the public money burden medical care of the medical treatment" is promulgated from Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare on April 10, 2006, and, as a general rule, from 2011 online request will be made mandatory in dentistry (it was afterwards in the direction that part of departmental order was revised on November 25, 2009, and (Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare law 151) [1], online became "I become a principle" from "complete imposition", and exempted you from mandatory about a part including the medical office of the old doctor and the small hospital).

For measures of this receipt online, development began for the purpose of making use of the merit of the ASP method, and offering a receipt computer to the member of dental association for low price. Development, industrialization was decided in the Japan Dental Association board of directors of October, 2008. I choose NTT DATA as a company and develop it to date.

Release was started on February 1, 2010 [2], and an official site of レセック was established on February 22, 2010 [3]. Is developed assuming the request in online and the electronic medium, but even paper receipt can make prints (but, in that case, it is necessary for to line up, and a dental clinic to perform the work of the substitute); [3]. The receipt computer has many things of the contract of the age unit, but, in レセック, it is moon contract to raise convenience [3]. The nickname of "レセック" invited public participation to the member of the dental association and was decided [5]. "The medical treatment pack" to use at the time of medical treatment input is customizable by oneself. I can match it with medical treatment-style of the dental clinic. There is "the レセック free evaluation version" which can experience レセック from the PC of home and the medical office.

On September 26, 2011, version up to "レセック 2" was carried out [6]. Improvement of the user interface, improvement of the screen display speed were accomplished, and the return receipt online re-request function that there was many of the request was added by a user [6]. Characteristic of レセック

It is cheap and is provided
It becomes the cheap service to adopt ASP method. All functions of レセック, telephone support by the Help Desk, VPN connection expense, maintenance expenses such as at the time of medical treatment reward revisions are included.
Use is simple and easy
In the case of conventional software-shaped レセコン of everyday backup work and a revision oneself because replacing it was necessary, but レセック is carried out on the data center side for ASP method collectively update work at the dental clinic of the software of the medical treatment reward is not necessary.
It is at low risk of the data extinction
Because it is saved to a server of the datacenter that patient information and the medical treatment information are solid, risks such as data corruption or loss such as a disaster or the theft are small.
Simple operability
PC in itself assumes the first user and comes to be able to perform most operation with a mouse. Remote (remote control) support is received in a Help Desk even if I do not understand operation.
