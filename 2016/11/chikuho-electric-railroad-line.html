Chikuho Electric Railroad Line (ちくほうでんきてつどうせん) is a pike of Chikuho Electric Railroad linking Chikuho-Nogata Station of prefecture Nogata-shi from Kurosaki-Ekimae Station of Yahatanishi-ku, Kitakyushu-shi, Fukuoka. There is not the official route name.

I use a streetcar-shaped vehicle, but this route is "the railroad" that the whole line is based on the railway business method. Until February, 2015, in house between Kurosakiekimae - Chikuho Nogata, the Kurosakiekimae - Kumanishi interval, it was the third kind railway company the second kind railway company Nishi-Nippon Railroad (following "Nishitetsu") Chikuho Electric Railroad. Originally this section took the form that Chikuho Electric Railroad ran in with a part of Nishitetsu Kitakyushu Line, but changed section to remain when Nishitetsu abolished it between Kumanishi - Orio of the Kitakyusyu line in 2000 on the railroad by the railway business method from the orbit by the orbit method. I received spin-off and consisted of Nishitetsu assets of Chikuho Electric Railroad formally on March 1, 2015, and the third kind railway business of Nishitetsu was abolished [1]. In third party group compiling information such as the light rail in the U.K., association of light rail traffic (Light Rail Transit Association: LRTA), I classify this route as a railroad equivalent to light rail.
