Duty

About the duty, it is determined by Coast Guard method Article 2 and Article 5, but is distributed below when I classify roughly.

  1. Guard duties: The territorial waters guard, an exclusive economic zone of Japan and the criminal investigation and the arrest (judicial police) of the suspect, the guard of the important harbor, ship that occurred in a certain ship or ship of the Japanese nationality outside the exclusive economic zone in that.
  2. Rescue duties: The search rescue of a ship, the plane which met with a disaster on the sea and the passenger, crew, the emergency conveyance from a remote island and the ship which are not equipped with a medical institution, the processing (prevention of fire extinguishing of the fire and outflow of the pollutant, the removal) of the accident ship. I include Japan Team for Disaster Relief duties.
  3. Marine information (waterway) duties: The making of the chart, the measurement of the tide, an investigation into seafloor topography.
  4. Traffic duties: I install a lighthouse, a beacon, a navigation support system and manage it to plan facilitation of the traffic on the seas.
  5. Other duties: The guard in the remote island. Because the crime case in the land was business of the police, the Coast Guard was not able to cope alone in the remote isolated island department either.
