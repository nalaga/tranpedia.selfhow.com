Throne war

In this item, I explain a shogi game of the Japanese shogi.
  • Please see "a throne war" (Go in Korea) about the throne war of the Go in Korea.
  • Please see "a throne war" (mahjong) about the throne war of the mahjong.

A shogi game of the shogi that the throne war (I chase I do not shirr it) is hosted by association of three local papers (Hokkaido Shimbun Press, Chunichi Shimbunsha (Chunichi Shimbunsha, Tokyo Shimbun), Nishi-Nippon Shimbun) and Kobe Shimbun, Tokushima newspaper publisher. It is performed every year from July through September. The winner of "the throne war seventh game" is called the throne and becomes a titleholder.

"Three cup Class B selection tournaments" developed in 1960 and were dissolved and became the fourth title.

Method

At the time limit, a qualifier, a challenger decision league, a challenger jump-off is for each four hours. The seventh game performs a sealed move in two days system of 8 hours at the end of the first day at the time limit.

Qualifier

Eternity throne

The shogi player who held the throne more than ten sittings in total or consecutive five sittings is given the eternity throne who is an eternity title.
