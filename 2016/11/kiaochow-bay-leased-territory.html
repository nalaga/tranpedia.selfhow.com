A map of the Shangdong Peninsula of 1912 when I was under power titled "Schantung und das Kiautschou-Gebiet" in Germany. There is the enlarged picture of the Kiaochow Bay leased territory in the top right corner.

The leased territory where Deutsches Reich owned the Kiaochow Bay leased territory (こうしゅうわんそしゃくち, Jiaozhou Bay) in the Shangdong peninsula South Coast in northern China. The peninsula of both sides of whole of Kiaochow Bay which heart-shaped, cut into the southeast of Kiaochow (a part of current Qingdao City) surface of the water and the entrance of the gulf was a domain. The area is 552 square km. The position is 120°14'E 44.3 seconds for 36 degrees N seven minutes 24.44 seconds. I existed from 1898 through 1914.

In the Kiaochow Bay leased territory, it was become "Kiaochow" "Kiauchau" "Kiao-Chau" and a Romaji in English in German based on pronunciation of "Kiaochow" in those days "Kiautschou". As an administrative center of the Kiaochow Bay leased territory, Germany built Qingdao (Tsingtau, current notation: Qingdao) in the peninsula of the gulf entrance east side.

Background

Consciousness to colony acquisition increased like other countries in Deutsches Reich which achieved unification in the times of the imperialism expansion of the 19th century, and this greatly influenced German colony construction in China. Furthermore, a characteristic of the German colony empire included an idea that it was ideal that the colony supported mother country economy. Therefore, I attracted attention very much as the export market of the German product, and China with much population became the target of the colonization. The thinker like Max Weber requests the government to adopt an aggressive colonial policy, too. It was thought that China market was the most relevant in world non-Europe market, and the opening and closedown became the matter of life and death again in those days for Germany for the Great Powers.

However, it was thought that world policy (de:Weltpolitik) was impossible without global military influence. For global military affairs influence, German navy fleet follows the profit of the time of peace of Germany as a leading figure of the gunboat diplomacy, and it is included in the wartime I defend paths of German trade, and to be able to interfere with paths of trade of the enemy country. Therefore, it was important that I built the naval base network of Germany in all parts of the world, and the home port of cruiser fleet, the Germany Orient fleet and the port where it was, and it was with call at a port ahead of ocean fleet (Hochseeflotte) in the Germany own country were necessary in China.

The securing of harbor in China was accompanied by the different purpose. In the German colony in China, it should have been in the advertising place of the German navy if I thought about strong strain out of Germany by the fleet reinforcement. The Kiaochow Bay leased territory was built towards a model colony from the beginning. As for all facilities and administrations, good efficiency, it was said to the thing which showed off an effective German colonial policy by far that it should be for a Chinese, the German nation and the world.

Occupation and lease by Germany

In 1860, an expedition fleet of the Kingdom of Prussia visited Asia, and the around Kiaochow Bay area was investigated at this chance. In 1861, the following day, it was signed a trade agreement of Prussia and 清. Ferdinand phone Richthofen recommends Kiaochow Bay as an ideal fleet base after the exploration from 1868 through 1871 in China.

The Alfred phone ティルピッツ admiral who was the commander of the German Orient fleet investigated this area personally at the time of 1896 after the Sino-Japanese War. What built the scaffold in China in comparison with Russian Empire and France which sold favor together for 清 looked for a delay, the area where did not still have the hand of other Great Powers by interference, and three Germany finally fixed on the Shangdong peninsula.

The case that two German propagators were murdered in 巨野県 (きょやけん, current 菏 swamp city) in western Shangdong on November 1, 1897 happened. This "鉅野事件" (Juye incident) gave an excuse of the aggression called "the protection of the German propagator" to Emperor German Wilhelm second. German Orient commodore phone ディーデリヒス (de:Otto von Diederichs) which was in Shanghai received the order of the Kiaochow Bay occupation operation start on November 7 before 清国政府中央 knew the details of this case (he will get the position of the Governor-General of military until March 7, 1898). On November 14, the German Marine Corps struck Kiaochow Bay on the pretext of the landing of the long-term voyage middle and land training and gave it an order for withdrawal than 1,000 清国兵 which was in the all the soldiers public office of 膠澳 without a battle and occupied the whole coastal line. 清国側 continued making useless efforts to withdraw this military unit. On April 6 six weeks later, this area was placed under protection in Germany publicly and opened as a treaty port on July 1, 1899. The population in the leased territory was 83,000 people at this point in time.

The last of the leased territory

Just after the World War I outbreak of war, Japan gave final note for Germany to return Kiaochow Bay leased territory to China. On August 23, 1914 of the deadline Japan to Germany; declared war (Japan and Germany war). I was going to make a cruise (the main force has already escaped at the time of the outbreak of war from Qingdao), and the German Orient fleet was defeated by the German own country by South American Falkland offing sea fight for fear of the confinement of the gulf. The Japanese military which struck the Shangdong Peninsula in September continued the battle with the land route Germany forces toward Kiaochow Bay, and there was a fight between the vessels inside and outside the gulf. As a result of fight of Qingdao from October 31, the Japanese military occupied Kiaochow Bay on November 7, 1914. In addition, the activity in the Indian Ocean of light cruiser Emden which got away from Qingdao just before the Japan and Germany war outbreak of war is famous.
