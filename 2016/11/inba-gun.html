Inba-gun (いんばぐん) is a county of Chiba (Shimousa country).

A population of 41,919 people, area 51.52km², population density 814 /km². (on October 1, 2016 population by estimate)

I include following two towns.

  • Shisui-machi (I do it and breathe it and wait)
  • Sakaemachi (it prospers and waits)
