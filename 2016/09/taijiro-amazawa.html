Taijiro Amazawa

Taijiro Amazawa (あまざわたいじろう, July 21, 1936 -) is a Japanese poet, a scholar of French literature, a juvenile literature writer, a translator. Kenji Miyazawa researcher. Tokyo University department of literature French literature department graduation. Professor emeritus at Meiji Gakuin University department of literature French literature department.
