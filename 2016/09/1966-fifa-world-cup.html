1966 FIFA World Cup (British: 1966 FIFA World Cup) is the eighth FIFA World Cup held in England from July 11, 1966 through July 30. Local England defeated West Germany in 4-2 in the final and accomplished championship of the fifth country in history. As the World Cup, a technical study group (the group which analyzes a game, and analyzes a technique and the tactics, a tendency, and makes the technical report every game and the generalization report of the meeting) was introduced for the first time by this meeting [1].
