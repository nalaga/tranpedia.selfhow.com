Sphere (sphere) [note 1] is Minako Kotobuki, Ayahi Takagaki, Haruka Tomatsu, a voice actor unit by Aki Toyosaki. Music lane position. Each is active as both a voice actor and a singer in individual treatment. Position label as the units is GloryHeaven.
