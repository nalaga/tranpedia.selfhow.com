The republican form of government nation where Republic of Mali (Malian きょうわこく), popular name Mali are located in West Africa. It is a landlocked country among Senegal in Guinea, the west in Burkina Faso, Cote d'Ivoire, southwest in Niger, the south in Algeria, the east in Mauritania, the north in the west. The capital is Bamako.

It is a dry zone except that a one-third north side of the country is a part of the Sahara, and only the Niger River coast where the remaining middle southern district just flows through the center to becomes the farming place.
