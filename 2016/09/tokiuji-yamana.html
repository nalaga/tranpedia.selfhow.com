Tokiuji Yamana (when a mountain a maggot) is a military commander, a feudal lord of north and south morning service charges from last years of Kamakura era. Muromachi Shogunate Military Headquarters head, a judge. Hoki・Izumo・Oki・Inaba・Wakasa・Tanba・TangoThe protection.

Life

As for father, Sei Yamana, mother are daughters of Shigefusa Uesugi. To a child teacher justice, justice, he winter, 氏清, 時義. Mother Kiyoko Uesugi of Takauji Ashikaga, Naoyoshi brothers acts as the cousin of the mother's side.

I was born in the house of the family who was the whole families of Nitta of Ueno. It is said that I lived a life of the people farmer according to "the difficulty Taihei note" that Sadayo Imagawa wrote, but because Yamana is the family from Kamakura Shogunate establishment and I bind relative by marriage relations together with Uesugi, it is hard to think with the low social position, and it is thought that 貞世 looked down on Yamana who is a rival as for this description.

I participate in estrangement from the Emperor Godaigo of Takauji Ashikaga, a fight of Minatogawa without obeying it to Yoshisada Nitta who is Soriyo of the Nitta clan from kinsman relations with Uesugi who is a relative by marriage of Ashikaga. I perform Masayuki Kusuki, a sweep of Nawa by a fight with the south morning and raise achievement by the Takasada Ennya subjugation of (1341) for / Ryakuo four years for founding a country two years, and it is Hoki, Izumo, the Oki protection by the success and was defeated the fight with Masatsura Kusunoki in (1347) for / Jowa three years for Shohei two years, but it is the Wakasa protection in the next year.

When opposition of Moronao Kono of (1350), Naoyoshi Ashikaga of the younger brother of 室町幕府初代将軍尊氏 and the Ashikagas butler develops for / Shohei five years in the Kano era first year and has disturbance of the Kano era, Mr. recommends teacher direct を at the start at time and participates in a coup d'etat of the Naoyoshi rejection, but I get away from Kyoto in December and belong in south morning and follow Naoyoshi who destroyed teacher direct を. When Naoyoshi dies in (1351) for / Shohei six years for Kano era two years, the following day, I turn into the general group at one time, but there is the opposition with Doyo Sasaki over the Izumo lief-guard and I raise an army for the Muromachi Shogunate in (1353) for / Shohei eight years for Fumikazu two years and chase Yoshiakira Ashikaga with Masanori Kusunoki and others in south morning to Izumo in attack, June and occupy Kyoto, but am recaptured in July.

After having withdrawn in the fief, Mr. time conforms to Tadafuyu Ashikaga who had influence in Kyushu in bastards of 尊氏 at one time and occupies Kyoto with Takatsune Shiba and Tadatsune Momonoi and others again (1354) in December for / Shohei nine years for Fumikazu three years, the following day, but withdraws. I extend influence taking advantage of confusion of the shogunal administration and, in San-in, fight against Norisuke Akamatsu of Harima afterwards.

Yoriyuki Hosokawa was appointed in the Shogunate by a governor-general of the Kanto area, and submission work was performed for Ouchi and Yamana if the fight with the south morning became in remission, and Mr. let justice visit the capital subject to relief of the fief (1363) at he winter and time of the son in August for / Shohei 18 years for estrangement, Sadaji two years from direct winter at time, and, as for Mr., as for the teacher justice, the justice was appointed for Hoki, the Tanba protection following Ouchi in the Muromachi Shogunate at Inaba, time in Tango, he winter at submission, time by the Mimasaka protection (changed in the justice of the second son later), and, as for Yamana, it was the protection of five countries. In addition, I was appointed to a judge and participated in shogunal administration. Yukiko Shibukawa of 義詮正室 and Yoshimasa Shiha who similarly submitted in the Shogunate, Hiroyo Ouchi and others were the military commanders of the anti-Yoriyuki group together in the Shogunate.

I die at (1371), 69 years old for / Oan era four years for Kentoku two years. I was buried in the Mitsutaka temple (現山名寺倉吉市厳城) of Hoki Daiyuzan, and teacher justice of 嫡男 inherited the back.

Mr. time made efforts in expansion of the own power taking advantage of the rivalry between north and south the Southern Dynasty and the Northern Dynasty and feudal lords and built Tauchi castle and the Utsubuki castle in Nijo Yamashiro, Hoki to Inaba, and, as for Yamana, it was power of the San-in district best before long. Five sons increased feudal tenure after death of Mr. time, too, but it is regarded as dangerous by the family to inherit the shogunate and is connected for a fight for later same tribe.

Person, anecdote

It is thought that it was made a cynical remark on once having submitted with Mr. time that was an enemy maintaining great power.

Because Mr. time talks about one's experience to children by "difficulty Taihei note", and even oneself who knew reason sometimes neglects ideas of rulers, there is the anecdote that I worried about when a degree is over, and it may be cautious of as for the descendant on the top. As for the truth, Yamana would be hunted down and killed in spite of being ignorance 20 years after death of Mr. time by the family to inherit the shogunate, and the power has been reduced.
