The women's soccer club where AS L fen Saitama (A S L fen Saitama, English: AS Elfen SAITAMA) belongs to the Japanese women's soccer league moving into action around Sayama-shi, Saitama.

In addition, AS of the team name meant A of the ridge rear that became a forerunner and Sayama, Saitama, (prefecture) western S, and L fen (Elfen) meant "a footballer" (in English eleven) who came from 11(elf) of the number in German, and I was named by a wish, "I wanted you to do feminine soccer" because I reminded you of L fin (elfin) which meant a fairy in English.

A team name becomes "ちふれ AS L fen Saitama" with the naming rights contract conclusion with ちふれ cosmetics from a season in 2016. [1]

The history

  • 1985： I start activity as a girl part of "Sayama-shi sports Boy Scouts ridge rear FC".
  • 1998： I participate in an empress cup all-Japan women's soccer championship as Kanto block representative.
  • 1991： I become independent as girl team "AS L fen FC" 12 years or older.
  • 2002： I participate in an all-Japan women's soccer championship as Kanto block representative. I join the Japanese women's soccer league. A name is changed to "AS L fen Sayama FC".
  • 2003： It is the fourth place in the fourth place, lower part league in a primary league. Participated in "the L1 entry team jump-off" of the four teams round robin, but the last place of one win two defeats.

  • 2004： With two part system of introduction of the Japanese women's soccer league, I belong to two copies of L leagues (later dianthus league Div.2).
  • 2010： I am promoted to the dianthus league (first division).
  • 2012： It becomes the ninth place in a league match, and it turns around, and it is defeated by 入替戦. It is demoted by the challenge league.
The details refer to "2012 Japan women's soccer league # going up and down case"

Furthermore, Ayumu Hara of the coach returned active play after player registration in July, and Shinobu Ono of representative Japanese girl joined it. I finish a league match with the second place and return to the dianthus league in one year.
  • 2014： I change a name by the reason of enlarging the place of the activity as a base throughout the prefecture in Sayama-shi to "AS L fen Saitama" [4].
  • 2015： Because the league match regular series exciting series became the tenth place (the last place) together, it is demoted by two copies of dianthus leagues.
  • 2016： I change the team name to "ちふれ AS L fen Saitama" with the naming rights contract conclusion with よりちふれ cosmetics on February 1 [1].
