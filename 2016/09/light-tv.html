コミュファ light TV

コミュファ light TV (コミュファ Hikari TV) is service of the cable TV which internet service provider "central part telecommunication" (コミュファ) of KDDI, the Chubu Electric Power group presents.

Table of contents

Summary

  • I start delivery on October 1, 2010.
  • It is a subsidiary of electric power company, and it is the optical fiber cable TV business which is next to Kyushu Electric Power "Kagoshima light TV", but I just utilize the TV tuner of the user without attaching the necessary set top box (exclusive tuner) in the case of the seeing and hearing of the cable TV as the point that is different from existing cable TV, and, "eo light TV of Kansai Electric Power, K-Opticom," it is to structure (the structure which is approximately equal to FLET'S TV light, light PerfecTV SKY PerfecTV) which can watch ground wave and digital broadcasting of BS by a pass-through method Shikoku Electric Power "ピカラ light てれび". It supports only a door-built house and is not available now in the apartment complexes such as apartment apartments. In addition, I offer service in the supply of electric power area of Tokyo Electric Power in Shizuoka.
  • I do not carry out the re-delivery of analog broadcasting and the communication satellite broadcasting of ground wave, BS. But I can watch some communication satellite broadcasting by the mixture with BS, CS antenna and the tuner in the home after having cut BS signal of コミュファ light TV by using the paid option of "the visit double support to entrust you".
  • In addition, there is not an indie channel unusually in the cable TV station.
