The cherry tree is the limited express train which West Japan Railway Co. (West Japan Railway) and Kyushu Railway Co. (Kyushu Railway Company) operate between Shin-Osaka Station - Kagoshima-Chuo Station of Sanyo Shinkansen, Kyushu Shinkansen. The color indicating the classification pinkness (■) [1].

Summary

I started service by Sanyo Shinkansen and Kyushu Shinkansen with Kyushu Shinkansen (Kagoshima route) whole line opening of March 12, 2011 and started direct driving of both Shinkansen at the same time [2].

"Sakura" greatly distributes it and is classified roughly to a system to conclude in system to run directly between Sanyo Shinkansen and Shin-Osaka Station - Kagoshima-Chuo Station of Kyushu Shinkansen and Kyushu Shinkansen including the Hakata Station - Kagoshima-Chuo Station interval. The train concluded only with the Sanyo Shinkansen is not operated and, as for the special delivery train to conclude in the line, is traveled as "Hikari".

It becomes 3 bookends of the "Mizuho" "cherry tree" "swallow" every special delivery classification by Kyushu Shinkansen, and, as for the nickname of "the cherry tree", it is the positioning (equal to "Hikari" in Tokaido, the Sanyo Shinkansen) of "Sanyo Shinkansen direct communication or the special delivery train to conclude in Kyushu Shinkansen section". There is the most driving number of "the cherry tree" in these 3 classification and, in substitution for "the swallow which traveled after the part opening of business," is traveled as a signboard train of Kyushu Shinkansen.

I follow the stop pattern of the conventional "Hikari rail star" from the process that started service as a train for "Hikari rail star" with the Sanyo Shinkansen. Consecutive number was used for the beginning of service start in Sanyo Shinkansen "cherry tree" and "Hikari rail stars", but it was separated than time schedule revision of March 17, 2012, and, as for the 540-580 level, it was the number for exclusive use of "a cherry tree".

Origin of the train name

Taking advantage of Kyushu Shinkansen opening the entire line, Kyushu Railway Company and West Japan Railway invited public participation for the train name which drove Kyushu Shinkansen and Sanyo Shinkansen directly from October 1, 2008 to November 30 (an adopted person hits the nickname, and they are alone, and Kyushu Railway Company presents 200,000 yen to the window). As a result, it wants to be announced on February 26, 2009 that the new train name assumes it "a cherry tree" [3]; [4]. The name of "the cherry tree" won 7,927 of them which became the most in 168,951 application total number and was chosen by being equal to "the Japanese beauty" that was a concept of the new vehicle N700 system 7000th level .8000 turn level developed for direct driving.

Service general condition

As for the service number according to the section in the daytime, it is operated two of them an hour between 1-2 of them an hour, Hakata Station - Kagoshima-Chuo Station between Shin-Osaka Station - Hakata Station. The train to turn down between Hakata Station - Kagoshima-Chuo Station is set in the Kyushu Shinkansen section. All the trains of the Sanyo Shinkansen section communicate Kyushu Shinkansen directly. The train running between Hakata Station - Kumamoto Station at the daytime was set once, but, as of March 15, 2014, is not traveled by the periodical train.
