Keihan Bus Hirakata office (けいはん bus ひらかたえいぎょうしょ) is an office of Keihan Bus in Hirakata-shi, Osaka.

The abbreviated designation written to the company car "Hira" as for the abbreviated designation printed by a bus card common throughout Keihan group is "枚".

The use of PiTaPa was enabled in jurisdiction from October, 2008.
