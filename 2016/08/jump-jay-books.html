A new book label of light Novell where jump Jay books (JUMP j-BOOKS) are published from Japanese publishing company, Shueisha origin. March, 1993 foundation of a periodical.

Summary

I was launched to collect a publication novel work of extra number "jump Novell" (from 1991 to 1999) of "the weekly boy jump". I publish it mainly on the novelization work of "weekly boy jump" system serialization comics, a jump novel rookie of the year title (old jump novel, nonfiction award) prize winner even now.

The publication work after the "jump Novell" suspension of publication has many newly written works, but there is the work which was published after I was placed in the magazine ("jump SQ"). The serialization of the novel work is carried out in the official site.

The jump J books editing post belongs to the jump comics publication editorial department of Shueisha [1]. As of 2015 the chief editor of (the eighth generation) Takanori Asada (June, 2014 ...) [2].

I make much of being "a character novel" for a style [3].
