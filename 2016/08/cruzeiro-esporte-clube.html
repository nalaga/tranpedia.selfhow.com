Cruzeiro エスポルテ クルーベ (Portuguese: Cruzeiro Esporte Clube) is F.C. basing on state capital Belo Horizonte of Brazil Minas Gerais.

Oswald オリヴェイラ which led Kashima Antlers to the J League 3 straight victory has ever taken office as supervision.

History

I was founded by a club name of SE palace tiger Italy (Sociedade Esportiva Palestra Itália) on January 2, 1921 by wops and accepted only a wop until 1925. The original club collar was white, green, red same as an Italian national flag.

Because Brazil arrived at the allied powers side in World War II, changing name was forced, and the organization which suggested Italy renamed the club to Cruzeiro Esporte Clube in 1942. The cruzeiro refers to the Southern Cross which is drawn to the Brazilian national flag. At this time, the club collar turned into blue and white, too.

The championship number of times by the Minas Gerais championship is prominent with rival Clube Atletico Mineiro of same Belo Horizonte. I won the championship for a national championship in Brazil for the first time in 2003. I won Kopa de Brazil and the state championship to this age, and it was in the club which achieved 3 crowns first in the history of soccer in Brazil. I win the championship in twice of 1976 and 1997, Kopa re-Berta dough reply in the international stage and win a South American champion.

In a club popular with the sixth in the Brazilian whole country, it is a club most popular in Minas Gerais [1].

The Mine irone (an official name: エスタジオ ゴベルナドール マガリャンイス focus) of the home stadium shares it with Clube Atletico Mineiro.

Rival

The biggest rival of the cruzeiro is Clube Atletico Mineiro basing on same Belo Horizonte. The play of these two teams is called スーペル クラシコ ミネイロ (the supermarket Derby of ミネイロ). In addition, there are rival relations with America Mineiro.
