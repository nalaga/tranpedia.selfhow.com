Process of the development

The M2 whippet tank which the United States developed before World War II was a superior car, and M2A4 whippet tank 36 輌 was performed a loan (only 4 輌 of them was only sent to Egypt, and the others deploy an own country) of in the British troops in June, 1941 by the Ren dories method, and a nickname of "Stewart" was attached. However, even this is insufficient for the 37mm antitank gun and will develop the new whippet tank which I strengthened to 38.1mm (1.5 inches).

As previously described, the important point of the improvement was put for the reinforcement of the armor, and body in itself was extended backward, too. The weight increased to 12.7 tons from 11.6 tons of the M2A4 whippet tank with increase of the armor thickness and upsizing of the body, but it was checked to make an instruction ring big, and to contact with the ground and considered increase of the grounding pressure. The armor was the rivet joining as well as an M2 whippet tank. A welding technology was unripe and was excusable choice, but when it was hit in head of the rivet in a shooting examination, a remaining part played it, and it flew, and it still became clear at that time to kill and wound crews in the car. Deployment and operational

The mass-produced M3 whippet tank was given to the Soviet Union, France, Australia, China as well as a weapon made in many other United States including the ally U.K. The British troops cast this car into a fight in the North Africa and were called by a nickname called "honey" with friendly feeling from high reliability.

Operation クルセーダー was exercised right after this car arrived at the North Africa, and this one car regiment (approximately 150 輌) participated. The M3 whippet tank was utilized as a substitute for cruise tank here, but heat, armor was short together and gave much damage. The M3 whippet tank possessed high reliability and was a whippet tank superior in mobility, but big lowering it 砲 not having been able to be equipped with the body and 履帯幅 were small, and there were the faults such as grounding pressure being high, the thing that a flying range had a short. Therefore, the battle duty in the North Africa was replaced by M4 shaman medium tank supplied newly, and the M3 whippet tank came to be invested in a reconnaissance duty.

M3A1 of the U.S. forces which fought after this in Tunisia challenged a German tank and started great damage, and the whippet tank of this class has been shown the limit of what a tank battle and a foot soldier supported to Germany forces partner. Furthermore, after mid-1942, new M5 whippet tank began to be deployed and was excluded from the front line equipment for U.S. forces in the form that was gradually pushed in 1943. M3A3 continued being used in the Free French and free Paulhan Dodgers, the British troops of the Burmese area, but I remove a gun turret of M3A3 in European British Federales, and there are many things remodeled into the ammunition truck shop gun tractor. In addition, it is remodeled into the Stewart レッキ reconnaissance car which after all removes a gun turret of M3A3 in the British troops of the Italian front, and lightweights armament only as a machine gun, and increased mobility.

On the other hand, the 192nd tank battalion (M3 whippet tank 54 輌), the 194th tank battalion (53 輌) were deployed in the Philippines at the time of the outbreak of war in the Pacific War. When the Japanese military struck Luzon on December 22, 1941, M3 whippet tank 15 輌 which was over this for interception fights with 95 sets of whippet tanks of the tank fourth regiment second squadron first platoon position. 95 sets of front armor of the M3 wanted to reject all the 37mm guns of the whippet tank, and 5 輌 could not act in bodily crash or 履帯切断 of the enemy platoon leader car, and (the armor accomplishment power of 94 sets of 37mm tank guns of 95 sets of whippet tanks was poor beyond comparison with the antitank gun of the general 37mm class) was repulsed. In addition, an M3 whippet tank (total number approximately 150 輌) of the second tank regiment position of the British seventh airplane former brigade position played an active part in the fight around Rangoon of Burma in February, 1942. The front armor of the M3 whippet tank could not penetrate with 94 sets of 37mm machine guns and tank gun of the powerless Japanese military, and the 37mm gun of the M3 was able to penetrate the armor of all Japanese tanks from a long distance adversely. 日本軍での鹵獲運用

 
日本軍に鹵獲されたM3軽戦車－1942年1月、フィリピンにて

太平洋戦線で鹵獲されたM3軽戦車は日本軍戦車より機動力・防御力が優れ、37mm戦車砲M6の攻撃力も九七式中戦車改の一式47mm戦車砲と変わらなかったために重宝された。なお、当時の日本軍は戦車開発において列強から取り残されつつあり、「日本が実戦に投入した最強の戦車は鹵獲したM3軽戦車」などというジョークが存在する[2]。

1942年（昭和17年）4月のコレヒドール島要塞攻略作戦にあたり、本土から急派された九七式中戦車改を中核として編成された臨時戦車中隊には九七式中戦車改2輌や九五式軽戦車4輌に混じって1輌のM3軽戦車も参加している。このうち、実際にコレヒドール島に辿り着けたのは中戦車2輌とM3のみだった。しかし予期せぬ戦車の投入によりアメリカ軍守備部隊は恐慌をきたし、結果的に難攻不落とされたコレヒドール要塞も同日中に陥落することとなった。なお上陸地点から台上に進出する際、登板能力に勝るM3が自力で登ることのできない中戦車を牽引したというエピソードも残っている。

また、インパール作戦に参加した戦車第14連隊では第1，2，3中隊がM3軽戦車を装備する軽戦車小隊を保有していたほか、作戦発動前には戦力強化のためにM3を装備する第4中隊が新たに編入された。作戦初期の1944年（昭和19年）3月には日本軍のM3軽戦車が英印軍のM3中戦車と戦闘を交え、これを撃破している。しかし無謀な作戦計画による補給の不足や強力な中戦車を前面に立てて戦闘を行う英印軍の前に戦車14連隊は壊滅した。
