The height of the pillar group was formed by the erosion that a temperature range of the year to become upheaval and 100 degrees of the Cambrian ocean basin at 300 meters from 150 meters brought. The whole area was appointed in Lena Pillars Nature Park (Ren Kie ストルブイ nature park) in 1995, and it was registered with a list of world heritages of UNESCO in 2012 [3].

Geography

The stone pillar group becomes from the layers such as limestone, marl (English version), dolomite, the slate from early period of Cambrian period to the middle and it is steep and is exposed by erosion [4]. The rocks of these types were formed generally in marine environment, and horizontal stratification and the vertical change show a state of transgression and the regression to the deep sea with the slate indicating there having been it.

The erosion that formed stone pillar group was called freezing rubble action (Cryoclastie), and difference of the difference of temperature that it was to summer and winter 100 degrees that an extreme continental climate brought brought it. By action of freeze and the thawing, the water drilled a narrow groove from the surface and tore off the most firm part of the rock little by little and formed the stone pillar group which remained now [5].

The pillar group of Rena is made much of as a Cambrian fossil production center [6].

World heritage

Lena Pillars Nature Park was set by the Sakha Republic in 1995. This park "was listed in the tentativeness list of the world heritage by a name natural park "pillar group (Nature Park "Lena Pillars") of Rena" in 2006 [7]. I was recommended first for the 33rd world heritage Committee (2009), but I received an advice of "the non-registration" then from the International Union for Conservation of Nature and Natural Resources (IUCN) which was a consultative body of world heritage Committee and withdrew it before the deliberation.

I was recommended some other time for the 36th world heritage Committee (2012) in 2011, but, at this time, the IUCN urged "registration postponement" [8]. What IUCN nominated for a reason is a defect of the integrity to come from non-adequacy of the setting and that of recommendation range. If it was a necessary part, and the reconsideration of the range was not performed for both proof of value of the nature beauty that the Russia authorities recommended and the value of the geologic paleontology, the Shinya shop river (Russian version) basin who was a branch of the range outside recommended by the judgment of the IUCN particularly the Lena did it when which value was hard to admit for a world heritage [9].

However, an advice was overturned in the 36th world heritage committee meeting held in St. Petersburg and became the registration with area (1,272,150 hectares) according to the original recommendation [10]. It is the 25th world heritage for a Russian world heritage (for the natural inheritance the tenth). But, in the value that Russia recommended at first, factor of the natural beauty was not accepted and became the registration only in value of the geologic paleontology. Pillar group of Rena is a place assumed the highlight in particular in Lena cruising from Yakutsk [1]. The Sakha Republic laid emphasis on sightseeing promotion of the pillar group, too, and the tourist was in a tendency to increase before world heritage registration [17].
