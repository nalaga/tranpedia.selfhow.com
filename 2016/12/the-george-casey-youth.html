The George William Casey youth (George William Casey, Jr., July 22, 1948 -) is an officer of the United States of America. I belonged to the U.S. Army and successively held the 36th Chief of Staff of the Army or commander-in-chief of living-in-Iraq multinational force. The last rank is a general.

Living-in-Iraq multinational force

Casey acted as a commander of the living-in-Iraq multinational force as a successor of the Ricardo Sanchez (English version) lieutenant general from July, 2004 to 2007 [1]. The aim of Casey was to let you succeed responsibility of the current problem and peace and order maintenance and authority smoothly to the Iraqi side. He reduced the activity of U.S. forces progressively while letting you train the cause of the authority as the commander, the Iraqi security force and shifted to the Iraq side in peace and order maintenance duty. When the foreign affairs station in the United States (English version) began support of the election for the government rebuilding in Iraq, I show the opinion "that the intervention that is large-scale so as to be pushy by the United States does not solve this state politics, security problem, and rather he just gives the reason of the revolt".

In 2005, Casey planned United States Armed Forces corps reduction in early 2006 in form to be parallel to the training of the Iraqi security force expecting parliamentary election (English version) of December leading to stability of Iraq. In panel discussion of August of the year, Casey showed some names of the military unit which might withdraw on the occasion of force reduction. He spoke it then saying that he succeeded a role in the Iraqi security force to be able to reduce the United States Armed Forces force of approximately 138,000 people approximately 30,000 by early 2006. U.S. President George W. Bush at the time performs severe attention for his remark saying that the claim of Casey is only a supposition to the last. In addition, the opposition between denominations deepened by bomb terrorism for the Al = Ascari temple (English version) of next Samarra, and the plan of Casey about situation stability by the coalition government establishment and the transfer of the peace and order duty was forced to a large correction [3]. In addition, I announce the statement to criticize military power reinforcement to Iraq in January, 2007 [4].

Chief of Staff of the Army

On February 10, 2007, Casey transferred a job of the Commander living-in-Iraq multinational force to David ペトレイアス general and left Iraq. On April 10, I take office as the 36th Chief of Staff of the Army as a successor of the Peter Shoemaker (English version) general [5].

During a period from April, 2007 when Casey worked as the Chief of Staff of the Army to 2011, the Army of the United States had approximately 11,000 staff and annual budget for 20 million dollars. He instructed two war in the term of office of 6 years and switched the army which assumed conventional war to the organization with the mobility suitable for war of the 21st century.

He tried for the reinforcement of the army and promoted expansion of the support for increase, the officer of the holding the post allowance for young officers (Retention Bonus) and the officer family, expansion of the support to the war disabled. In addition, I call education improvement of the high-quality officer corps, the adoption of the rotation-style front development program like the Marine Corps, army guardsman, and achievements of Casey include remedial education of the army reserve duty.

In November, 2009, I received outbreak of the shooting rampage (2009 Fort Hood shooting incident (English version)) by the psychiatrist ニダル Malik Hasan (English version) major of the Muslim, and Casey told, "I can call myself the great shame if this case was great tragedy, and our variety was violated again" saying that it was "true tragedy" that "variety" (diversity) was injured [6]. By the interview of February, 2010, I tell, "it will be a great mistake if I hide big power, and our variety that is variety of not only the army but also our country shivers with this terrible tragedy, and our variety is sacrificed" [7]. Received criticism later saying that such a remark was excessive consideration to so-called political correctness [8]; [9].

On April 11, 2011, I succeed the Chief of Staff of the Army's post to General Martin Dampsey and retire from the army. The military retirement ceremony of Casey was held in Fort Myer (English version).

I live in Massachusetts シチュエート (English version) after military retirement [10].
