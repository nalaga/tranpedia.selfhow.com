Indication

It is a series name of the small jetliner which Brazilian plane manufacturer, エンブラエル company produce エンブラエル E-Jet (Embraer E-Jet) and sell. The constitution model of this series has little standard number of seats; is エンブラエル 170 (78 seats), エンブラエル 175 (86 seats), エンブラエル 190 (104 seats), four kinds of エンブラエル 195 (110 seats) sequentially. Is classified in a regional jet [1], but [2] [3], エンブラエル 195 of the maximum during the series are setable to 124 seats with the number of seats, and there is the viewpoint that is equal to smallest model and airbus A318 of Boeing 737 in body size [6]; [2].

Process of the development

50 seats of エンブラエル company received a successful thing for industrialization of ERJ 145 which was the regional jet of the class, and started the development of more than 70 jetliners, and announced the development program of first model ERJ170 in February, 1999 [6]; [2]. Received ordering first in June of the year, and the production of the first issue machine began, and the roll-out of the first issue machine was performed on October 29, 2001 (at this verge, a name was changed from "ERJ170" to "エンブラエル 170"); [6]. E170 carried out maiden flight on February 19, 2002 119 days after the first public exhibition, and acquired Brazil, FAA, a TC of EASA in 2004, and the delivery to a customer was started, too [6]; [2] [15] [16].

In E175 which was a body extension model of E170, maiden flight was carried out in June, 2003 [6]. Acquired a Brazilian TC in 2004, and got the TC of FAA in EASA, 2006 in January, 2005 [17]; [18]. The first delivery to a customer was carried out for Air Canada in July, 2005 [6].

Maiden flight of E190 which was the model that made E175 big more continuously was carried out on March 12, 2004 and was exhibited to the public first in Farnborough international aviation service show [19]. Acquired the TC from EASA in FAA, 2006 in Brazil, September of the year in August, 2005 [20]; [21]. The delivery first to a customer was performed for JetBlue Airways in 2005 [2].

The maiden flight of E195 of the series greatest model was carried out on December 7, 2004 and was shown by Paris airshow [5]. I acquired a TC of EASA in Brazil, July of the year in June, 2006 [22], and the first delivery to Flybe was carried out in September of the year [5]. I acquire the TC of FAA in June, 2007, the following day [23].

The E170/E175 series is the E-Jet series of the small one. E170 and E175 compete with Bombardier CRJ-700 and Bombardier CRJ-900 directly. In addition, I competed with the Bombardier Q400 turboprop. Similarly, I aim at the substitute demand in the market ruled over in BAe 146 and フォッカー 70 for early similar design.

J-AIR of the JAL group introduced the E170 type machine for the first time as a Japanese airline and let the domestic リージョナル route departing from and arriving at Komaki Airport go into service from February, 2009. In addition, I introduce E175 as a main opportunity and navigate Fuji Dream Airlines now other than E170. E170 and 175 are equipped with the CF34-8E engine of 14,200 pounds of thrusts (62.28 kN) made in GE, アビエーション.

The E190/195 series comprises the large main wing of the new development and new CF34-10E engine of large-scale tailplane and thrust 18,500 lb (82.30 kN) in E170/175 which largely extended the body. These planes compete with Bombardier CRJ-1000. Furthermore, I compete with DC-9 which is a smaller main line passenger plane, Boeing 717-200, 737-500/-600, airbus A318 and the Bombardier C series under development in 100 seats of classes.

The launch customer of E190 placed an order for 100 planes in JetBlue Airways of the low-cost carrier based in New York and placed an order with 100 options. Flybe of the British low-cost carrier placed an order for E195 14 and placed an order with 12 options [26]. Jay air (Japan Airlines group) placed an order for an E190 type machine for machine parts update use at the airline in Japan, and a first issue machine was delivered in April, 2016.

Because the 190/195 series is relatively big in エンブラエル E-Jet, there is the airline adopting plural classes (seat). As an example, Air Canada installs nine seats of business class and 84 seats of economy class in 45 E190 which are an original plane member. In the case of Japan, E190 of the Jay air sets up 15 seats of class J and 80 seats of coaches [27].
