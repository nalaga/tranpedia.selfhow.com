The Orr DOS appears in the history as one of the toe men (the tribal aggregate which tribe (advantageous) gathered, and was formed) of the association of Mongolian tribe moving into action in Mongol Plateau in the about 15th century.

The name of the Orr DOS assumed "オルド" which meant "the Imperial Court" by Mongolian, テュルク word the etymology, and, in this group, 奉祀 did the Imperial Court of founder Genghis Hahn of Mongol Empire during the lifetime as a mausoleum of Genghis. The head of the group called itself dinon, but this was stationed in Mongol Plateau in the original times, and title "King Susumu" of the imperial family who protected a mausoleum of Genghis Hahn was taken in for Mongolian. Therefore, it is thought that an Orr DOS part is the group which assumes original King Susumu territory the origin [1].

In the original times, there was the mausoleum of Genghis Hahn in the KEL Ren abandonment area in northeastern Mongol Plateau, and ancestors of the Orr DOS region did nomadism in this area, too, but, in the about 15th century, the Orr DOS region went south with a mausoleum and entered the flexion of the Yellow River which the Han race got with "Kato" from ancient times and emigrated to the plateau called the Orr DOS Heights later.
