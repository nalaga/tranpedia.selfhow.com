Republic of Sierra Leone (Sierra Leonean きょうわこく), popular name Sierra Leone are Commonwealth of Nations member nations in a republican form of government nation located in western part, Atlantic coast of West Africa. It is bordered by Liberia to Guinea, the southeast to the north, and the southwest faces the Atlantic. The capital is Freetown.

It was in a colony of the U.K. as an emigration place of blacks freed from Egyptian bondage in 1808 and became independent in 1961.

Under the influence by the civil war that continued more than approximately ten years, it is one of the countries (I report 46 years old, WHO in 2014) which an average life span has a shortest now in the world (the Southern Africa countries where an AIDS evil is serious occupy the low rank of the average life span elsewhere).

History

Slave hunting was often performed in this area over the first half from the 16th to the 19th century, and the shore region was dotted with British small forts for a European and American base.

Colony in the U.K.

Freetown of 1856

The colony was moved in 1808 to the Royal Family management bottom. When a British assembly approved a law to be illegal by slave trade in 1807, in Freetown, it was in the base of the fleet which supervised the slave trade. Most of slaves with freedom remained behind in Freetown by a fleet, and the colony gradually spread. In addition, these freedmen make mixed blood in Freetown; called the Creole is new; racial. The colony escalated a domain through cession of the land and various treaties of friendship from local chiefs by 1861 and came to do whole Sierra Leone peninsula with a domain.

A faller bay university was established in Freetown in 1827, and the Creole played an active part as a missionary and a teacher in not only Sierra Leone but also other British colonies and came central in politics and trade in this area in the 19th century. On the other hand, the U.K. escalated power in the inland from the 1880s, and an inland area became the British protectorate formally in 1896. The Sierra Leonean protectorate and colony were sorted definitely politically. A revolt happened against a small housing fee introduced into the protectorate in 1898. When the 20th century began, the merchant of the creole could not compete with the foreign merchants such as the Lebanese whom I went into from the foreign territory, and the place of the activity came to be limited to Freetown. Furthermore, becoming it went ahead through the bureaucratic European of the colony, and the creole lost most of initiative in the politics.

A movement for racial liberation began as a leader in バンコーレ Brightman and Wallace Johnson of Dr. creole after World War I in Freetown. After World War II, the protectorate of the inland was given the right to vote, and Sierra Leonean Populist party (SLPP) which represented the profit of the protectorate as the party leader in doctor Milton circle fellows in 1950 was formed. SLPP tore the colony nation meeting (NCCSL) of Brightman and Johnson and others in Sierra Leone in the election of 1951, and SLPP won a great victory in the election of 1957, and it was Sierra Leone Prime Minister Hatsuyo, and I dispatched London delegation in 1960, and the circle fellow required Sierra Leonean full independence to admit.

I received democratization, and the Joseph rhinoceros do peach major general who became a successor of Stevens introduced political pluralism in 1990, but I received support of National Patriotic Forces of Liberia (NPFL) which led Charles tailor of neighboring country Liberia in 1991, and a unification revolution front (RUF) that led アハメド フォディ Sanko rose in arms, and Sierra Leone civil war broke out. The government was not stable, and RUF expanded the power afterwards in the chance.

The civil war was not settled though democratic election was held in 1996, and Ahmad Tejan Kabbah took office as the President and Johnny pole coRoma woke up a coup d'etat in 1997 and established a military regime, and it was in coalition government with RUF. However, the global community repelled this and the West African Economic Community (ECOWAS) military invaded and attacked to Sierra Leone in 1998 and overthrew the co-Roma Administration and brought back the cover government. The cover government was opposed to RUF again, and RUF let power spread until I influenced two-thirds of the country. Although it seemed that I ceased fire by Lome peace agreement (English version) of July, 1999 whether you began to go to the recovery of the order, Sanko took office as the Vice President and maintained power.

United Nations Sierra Leone mission (UNAMSIL) was dispatched on October 22, 1999, but capital Freetown control case of RUF where 500 personnel required of UNAMSIL are taken hostage on May 1, 2000 occurs. Sanko was restricted in Freetown on May 16 by the hand of citizens. In June, it is en:Operation Khukri. RUF is weakened rapidly afterward, and peace and disarmament of RUF succeed on January 18, 2002. The civil war terminated on March 1.

Sanko which was a leader of RUF dies in a hospital in Freetown in 2003. A Liberian tailor ex-President prosecuted on the charge of war crimes on March 29, 2006 was restricted in Nigeria of the exile and was transferred to Sierra Leone.

Democratization

Ernest co-Roma of the APC defeat Solomon Berea of ruling party SLPP in a presidential election on September 17, 2007 and take office as the new president. Since civil war end, the change of government by the first presidential election was realized. Incumbent Ernest co-Roma were reelected with a vote of the majority in a presidential election on November 17, 2012 [2].

Political

The national polity is the republican form of government nation which assumes the President the sovereign. The President is the head of the executive branch and is picked in election once in five years. The President appoints a cabinet minister and unifies it. The most recent presidential election was carried out in November, 2012.

In the legislature, the number of the seats is 124 seats from the assembly of the unicameral system. 112 seats are picked with a presidential election, and 12 remaining seats are supplemented by the President.

According to the statistics of IMF, the Sierra Leonean GDP of 2013 is 4,900 million dollars. It is 805 dollars, and there is the per person GDP in a standard not to arrive in 10% of world average either. [1]

Sierra Leone is the export country which assumed diamond digging a base since a diamond was discovered in 1930. However, most diamonds are smuggled out at the same time. The southwestern part is the area with many reserves of the diamond, and a civil war occurs over this in the past (I refer to dispute diamond # Sierra Leone).

The Wellington industrial area in the suburbs of Freetown was the area that was developed in 1960 by the Sierra Leonean government to begin production as part of a plan of the domestic economic development, and from the 1960s through the early 1970s this industrial area was one of the important economic areas of Sierra Leone. The beer company Sierra Leone beer limited which produced famous beer "star beer" in Sierra Leone was established in this industrial area in October, 1961.

After civil war end, the government lays emphasis on development of the diamond industry again, and the President of Ernest co-loma whom I took office as in 2007 is going to bring up diamond digging and abrasion industry.

Other than a diamond, it is a mineral bauxite and rutile (rutile), zircon-producing country. As for the bauxite, digging is begun a Sierra Leonean ore since 1963 by a metal company. The digging of the rutile containing titanium began in 1967, but was canceled in 1971. However, Sierra till company reopened production in 1979.

Rice, Elaeis guineensis, a peanut, coffee, cocoa are performed by the agriculture mainly. The fishery is prosperous in Freetown Peninsula.
