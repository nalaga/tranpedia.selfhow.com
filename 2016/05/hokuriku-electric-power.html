I use an abbreviated designation of Hokuriku Electric Power (ほくでん) in the business area, but use Hokuriku Electric Power (りくでん) for distinction with Hokkaido Electric Power out of the area. Summary

Business area (three prefectures painted in blue) of Hokuriku Electric Power

It is prosperous, and there are many influential local electric power companies, and hydraulic power generation development exists by the geography condition that the Hokuriku district contacts with the central part mountainous area from prewar days, and there is the history that promoted the development of the electricity many consumption type industry such as the electrical refining in an area by supplying inexpensive large quantities of the electricity.

Therefore, Hokuriku Electric Power was able to be established as a company independent of other blocks even if I kept the independent position as the local electricity block under the postwar electricity control for wartime and faced it at the time of electricity company reorganization in 1951 again.

Because most of the conventional hydroelectric power stations built in Shogawa and the Kurobe River were delivered to Kansai Electric Power after the war (this was because Japanese electricity developed it, but was considered to be an exception except upper reaches) from a junction with the Takahara River among the Miyakawa (main streams about the Jinzu River), I lacked electricity remarkably, and dam development in the Jinzu River and the Jouganji River was performed from 1950s through 1960s and was built the hydroelectric power station a lot by the 1980s. Therefore, the weight of the hydraulic power generation tends to be relatively big. Because the consumptions of the electricity increased at the side, the development of the thermal power station was pushed forward by progress at the same time (the Toyama thermal power station first unit completed in 1964 is the first among the company) and was able to find electricity as I was in excess. I managed the company's first nuclear power plant in 1993 and started, but it was introduction late nationwide because I took the posture not to accept nuclear power generation once.

I abolished a timeworn thermal power station for the purpose of breaking off a deficit in 2001 and 2004, and Toyama thermal power station 1-3 machine, the Fukui thermal power station first unit were abolished. All these were oil heat, and the Fukui thermal power station was brought into question as a cause of the air pollution in particular. In addition, the Shiga Nuclear Power Plant second unit is completed in 2006 and sells power in Kansai Electric Power and Chubu Electric Power for the purpose of supplying it to a low price more in an area and does it with an income.
